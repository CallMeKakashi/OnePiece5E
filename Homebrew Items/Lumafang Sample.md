# Lumafang Sample

**Wondrous Item, Very Rare (volatile)**  
A glowing vial of molten-gold serum.

When injected (as bonus action):

- Gain +2 to attack and damage rolls, +10 ft speed, resistance to fire and psychic for 1 minute.
- At end of each turn, take 5 (1d10) damage.
- After effect ends, DC 15 Con save or gain a level of exhaustion.

> Future tie-in: refined version shows up in Project Wonderland.
