## Blood and Brine - Session 2 Summary

- **A day after leaving Port Orlin**, the party sails to avoid Germa 66.  
- A **thick mist** rolls in, followed by the sound of **creaking wood**.  
- **Astor** notices a **5-sailed ship** he thinks he recognizes.  

### The Ghost Ship
- They board the ship, finding **bones with no clothes** and **waterlogged surroundings**.  
- The **captain's quarters** are blocked. **Astor** and **Ronan** break it open, releasing a flood of water.  
- No clues are found, only an eerie **"shirororo"** laugh as the ship **sinks**.  

### The Island and Lab
- Their boat mysteriously stops near an **island**.  
- A **purple glow** emanates from the fog.  
- They approach the light, finding a **panel, metal door, and massive walls**.  
- The letters "**M.A.**" are visible, but the rest are **scratched off**.  
- A voice welcomes them and invites them inside.  

### Puzzle Room 1 - The Clown's Questions
- The first room contains a glowing **panel**.  
- A distorted image of a **clown-like figure** appears.  
- The clown asks **nonsensical yes/no questions** with **green and red buttons**.  
- They answer wrong, resulting in **Astor being shot**.  
- Realizing the clown is **just stupid**, they start answering the “wrong” answers to progress.  

### Puzzle Room 2 - Tile Puzzle
- The next room has a confusing **tile puzzle** with knight and rook symbols.  
- **Ronan** figures it out but takes **damage** while solving it.  
- The team follows suit, enduring **more damage** before the second door opens.  

### Boss Fight - Caesar’s Clone
- The party enters a large room with a **suspended pod**.  
- The **clown clone** attacks using:
  - **Poison**
  - **Stunning gas**
  - **Fear-based attacks**
  - **Electric gauntlets**  
- **Pasha** throws one of the mysterious syringes found in Session 1.  
- **Astor** uses it, transforming into a **pseudo-Sulong** form, dealing massive damage.  
- **Ronan** grapples Caesar, landing successful attacks.  
- The party continues their assault, bringing Caesar to **half HP**.  
- Sensing defeat, Caesar tries to escape.  
- Failing to stop him, the clone **self-destructs**, knocking out **Pasha and Linn**.  

### Escape and Discovery
- The lab begins a **self-destruct countdown**.  
- They loot weapons, and **Ronan** grabs a **metal claw weapon**.  
- On their way out, **Astor** finds a **glowing object** on the ground.  
- The party boards their ship as the **lab explodes**.  

### The Compass
- Astor examines the object — **a compass with Blackhand Cane’s sigil**.  
- He urges the crew to **follow where it points**.  
- **Ronan disagrees**, while the others reluctantly comply.
