# Grand Line Tribune — 16th Sun, 1478 (Evening)

*"Truth Rides the Waves" — Price: 3,000 Beli*

## MARINE CAPTAIN RESCUED AFTER TWO MAJOR NORTH BLUE INCIDENTS

> Lex Imperia Prison Raid and Royal Navy Channel Ambush Shake Marine Forces

Marine forces throughout the North Blue remain on high alert following two separate large-scale incidents involving the notorious **Lunarfold Pirates**.

The first incident occurred at Noosehold and Lex Imperia, where reports confirm that the Lunarfold Pirates assaulted a Marine prisoner transport operation in order to free the dangerous spy known only as **Zim**.

During the chaos that followed, prison systems throughout Lex Imperia were breached, resulting in mass civilian casualties and widespread panic across the island.

Captain Calder Voss of Marine Unit G-4.5 was taken hostage during the incident before later being rescued by Marine forces. Officials confirm he remains under intensive medical care due to severe injuries sustained during the attack.

Before Marines could fully recover from the disaster, a second assault occurred days later within the Royal Navy Channels leading toward Lex Imperia.

This time, Marine escort ships transporting high-risk prisoners were ambushed by the Lunarfold Pirates alongside an unidentified hostile entity.

Among those freed during the attack was the former second-in-command of the infamous Red Maw Pirates.

Surviving Marines describe an overwhelming aerial assault involving multiple flying Devil Fruit users. One combatant was positively identified as **Malphas** of the Lunarfold Pirates.

The confrontation also resulted in the shocking defeat of rising Marine rookie Commander Leon of G-9, whose reputation had rapidly grown in recent months. Commander Leon is currently recovering from extensive injuries.

Marine officials additionally credited Captain Drez Crown of G-4.5 for saving numerous Marine lives during the ambush.

In direct response to the escalating threat posed by the Lunarfold Pirates, Commodore Briggs has officially approved the long-rumored **Arms Initiative** in partnership with Mugen Industries, signaling a major military escalation within the region.

Vice Admiral Horus has yet to comment publicly, as he continues attending the mourning ceremonies of former Admiral Graff Bolt.

Updated bounty posters for all known Lunarfold members accompany this issue.

## DECIBELLA UNDER MARINE PROTECTION

> Riff Sin Killed During Failed Peace Conference

The political crisis on Decibella has taken another dramatic turn.

Following the recent coup led by Riff Sin, Commodore Briggs arranged a peace conference in an attempt to stabilize the island and negotiate a peaceful transition of authority.

According to official Marine reports, the meeting deteriorated after Riff Sin allegedly launched an attack against the Commodore during negotiations.

Marine officials claim Briggs was forced to take immediate defensive action to protect both himself and his personnel.

The confrontation ended with the **death of Riff Sin**.

In the aftermath, the Marines have placed Decibella under temporary protection while discussions regarding the island's future continue.

As an interim measure, **Lady Siofra Anthem** has been appointed temporary ruler until a permanent governmental structure can be established.

While some citizens have welcomed Marine intervention, others fear Decibella may now fall directly under World Government influence.

Tensions across the island remain high.

## BLOOD ON NOOSEHOLD

> Lex Imperia Prison Breached — Red Light District Massacred

Disaster struck the prison island of Lex Imperia this week following a catastrophic security failure that left hundreds dead.

Officials confirm that large sections of the prison complex were opened during an attack whose perpetrators remain unidentified.

While details are scarce, witnesses report widespread panic as inmates flooded through the city streets.

The violence did not end there.

The following night, the island's infamous red-light district became the site of a horrifying massacre as a feral beast reportedly tore through civilians and Marines alike.

Survivors describe the creature as:

- Massive in size
- Covered in blood
- Moving "like a wild animal"
- Resistant to gunfire

Several eyewitness sketches are included with this issue of the Tribune.

The attacks have caused significant instability among the noble houses tied to Lex Imperia.

Most notably, the Valehart Family has announced plans to relocate to the East Blue alongside their distant relatives, the Vellencruz Family, in what officials describe as a "temporary strategic withdrawal."

Many fear these events mark the beginning of larger unrest spreading across the seas.

> Marines Respond to Rising Chaos

Marine Headquarters has released a massive wave of updated bounty posters connected to the recent incidents involving:

- The Lunarfold Pirates
- Escaped criminals from the convoy assaults
- Suspected attackers tied to Lex Imperia
- Known affiliates involved in the Decibella conflict

Officials warn that "the balance of power across the Four Seas is rapidly shifting."
