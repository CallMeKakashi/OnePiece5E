# East Blue Herald — Blackhand Shows His True Colors

**By Morgans — East Blue Correspondent**

## NORTH BLUE IN CHAOS AS BLACKHAND RAMPAGES!

Reports have flooded in from the North Blue: the infamous "Blackhand" Cane is rampaging across islands, leaving entire towns in chaos — and then vanishing without a trace! Even the Marines seem baffled by this sudden and violent spree.

Meanwhile, shocking reports arrive from the East Blue. The peaceful town of Ambercrest has been thrown into chaos by agents believed to be acting under Blackhand's orders. Our correspondent, Morgans went to the scene and gathered testimonies from the shaken populace.

## Voices From Ambercrest

**Two bounty hunters, faces streaked with tears:**

Sobbing uncontrollably "They killed our boss! We just wanted to talk… These guys are monsters!"

**A grumbling barkeep with a mug in hand:**

"Weird folks. One of them just cooked for me for free. They even asked to keep their ship stocked at the docks. Oh, and they took that drunk troublemaker **Linn** with them. Strange bunch..."

**A group of "critizens", pale and trembling:**

"Monsters! They… they ate the **fish dude**! We went back after they tried to kill us. We found bits of his shell in a box. Just terrifying..."

## Shopping District Shock

**An irate old sculptor wiping dust from cracked statues:**

"Those ruffians… disrespectful beyond words! They broke parts of the floor and rummaged through my precious work. My statues are ruined!"

**A sweating merchant fidgeting with his coin purse:**

"I—I didn't know they sold me stolen goods! Oh, I'm in so much trouble… That white-haired one was terrifying, but the polite red-furred one was surprisingly courteous. Wait, what? No, I didn't mean that. I mean to say pure evil…!"

## A Word from Marineford

In response to these events, Vice Admiral **Horus "The Maw"** made a chilling statement:

"He finally grew some balls, eh? Here's something for him and his crew: try to set foot in the Grand Line and I'll devour their ships whole."

With these alarming developments, questions swirl around Blackhand's true ambitions. Ruthless warlord? Or something else entirely? While rumors spread like wildfire, one truth is clear: Blackhand's flag now casts a shadow across both North and East Blue.

## A DARK ALLIANCE? SIXFOLD CONNECTION RUMORS

Perhaps the most shocking development: insiders allege that a member of the notorious mercenary group Sixfold **The Mad Doctor Linus** has now allied themselves with Blackhand. The implications are staggering — the Sixfold have long maintained a tenuous, secretive treaty with the Marines to avoid open conflict.

What does this mean for that fragile peace? Could it ignite tensions not just in the East Blue, but across the entire world? If true, this alliance may signal the birth of a new, terrifying power bloc capable of challenging even the World Government.
