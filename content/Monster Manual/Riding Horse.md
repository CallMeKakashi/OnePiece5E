# Riding Horse

## Visuals

![[Attachments/monsters/riding-horse.webp|Token]]

**Look:** Stock **riding horse** — brown bay coat, saddle-ready build, strong legs built for road travel; generic mount token (not **[[Pegasus]]**, B.O.B's summoned steed).

*Exported once from Foundry actor `zvPyMf70zmkqde20` (`systems/dnd5e/tokens/beast/RidingHorse.webp` — stock token). Duplicate actors on Foundry: `m3Bggi1x0UUyCSKB`, `wUQHs7VLGmItA1fR`. Reuse for any unnamed party or NPC mount.*

## Role

**Generic party mount** (CR 1/4) — ordinary riding horses the **[[Lunarfolds]]** used for overland travel after the **Decibel Decree** arc (guard horses commandeered in **[[Sessions/Session 14 - The Decibel Decree|Session 14]]**; wrangled again in **[[Transcripts/Episode 17 - Agony of Choas|Episode 17]]** with Roma's whistle). **Not named NPCs** — interchangeable `Riding Horse` stat blocks for the crew's horses, distinct from B.O.B's magical **[[Pegasus]]** mount.

## Appearances

- **[[Transcripts/Episode 14 - The Decibel Decree|Episode 14]]** — [[Baptiste]] takes a guard horse; party ties mounts when entering tight spaces.
- **[[Transcripts/Episode 17 - Agony of Choas|Episode 17]]** / **[[Transcripts/Episode 22 - The Missing Piece|Episode 22]]** — Horses travel with the crew between set pieces.
- **[[Sessions/Session 23 - Choice for life|Session 23]]** / **[[Transcripts/Episode 23 - Choice for life|Episode 23]]** — **Killed in the naval fight** vs the Lex Imperia fleet: one horse tied below deck is slain when a boarding beast attacks the nearest target; **both party horses dead** by the time the battle ends (Roma later cooks horse meat in the galley).
