# Carrion Vulture

## Visuals

![[Attachments/monsters/carrion-vulture.png|Portrait]]

**Look:** Large **vulture** with stylized purple wings, bald white head, hooked beak, and a wild crest of **spiky orange-red** feathers; sharp black talons. Cave-nester silhouette — reads almost anime-bird (South Bird energy) but still a carrion predator.

## Role

**Natural** flying predators (not Mugen / Nikolai work). Same **Brook Town ravine** as the **[[Ashscale Basilisk|Ashscale Basilisks]]**, but in a **lightless cave section**: open caverns, **flowing water**, **nest burrows** in the walls, and abandoned **man-made bridges** and structures—suggesting explorers used the caves until the vultures took over.

**Diet:** Mainly **[[Lamprey Horror|Lamprey Horrors]]** in the cave streams (competes with **[[Crystalback Scorpion|Crystalback Scorpions]]**, which hunt lampreys when humans are scarce).

**Ecology:** Shares ceilings and waterways with **Crystalback Scorpions** — vultures nest in burrows; scorpions cling above and ambush from the glow.
