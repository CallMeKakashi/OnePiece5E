# Red Maw Beast

## Visuals

![[Attachments/monsters/red-maw-beast.png|registry-image]]

**Look:** **Bat–human fusion** — hunched pink-red raw flesh, no visible eyes; wide flat head with huge **screaming maw** of yellow teeth, bat-like pointed ears, and dark nostril slits; sparse wiry fur on shoulders; thick fleshy tail. Reads as a failed Nikolai amalgam still close enough to human DNA to hurt.

*Exported once from Foundry actor `uvt8MOTs42FkpUjW` (`Windows-sync/Mafia Arc/token_4 (1).png`). Generic portrait for any **Red Maw Beast** from the airship crash — not a named NPC.*

## Role

**Nikolai / Mugen airship experiment** (CR 5) — forced **bat + human** fusion from the same aberrant line as the **[[Aberrant Abomination]]** and **[[Carrion Stalker|Carrion Stalkers]]**. Forensics would still read human DNA in the mix. Deployed from the **crashed airship** as part of Nikolai's **distraction** while covert island work continued (**[[Cave Render]]** mine plot).

**Blood Frenzy** and **Aberrant Regeneration** match the other crash mutates. Use as a bruiser in the same set piece as the Abomination and stalker pack — not the triple-head boss, but a heavy hitter that finished wounded survivors.
