## Role

**Generic rookie crewmate** (CR 1) for **[[Gin Guiseppi]]**'s grassroots pirate crew — the **Pump-Up Pirates**. Gin is the youngest [[Guiseppi Family]] sibling; he wants out of the family business and onto the open sea instead of mafia turf wars. This stat block is interchangeable deck muscle (dagger, leather, Enthralling Panache) for recruits who sail under Gin's flag — **not a named NPC**.

The family does not know about the crew except **[[Whisky Guiseppi]]**, who keeps Gin's secret. Contrast **[[Mafia Grunt]]** for on-the-books Guiseppi street soldiers still tied to Rum's organization.
