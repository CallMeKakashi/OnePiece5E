## Role

**Lunafang-mutated farm bull** from the **West Town** incident (**Juniper Islands**). A farmer brought **faulty fertilizer** laced with **[[LunaFang Serum|Lunafang]]** ([[Dr Nikolai Tesla]]'s Blues-wide spread); a child dumped it into the animal feed. The bull became a charging bruiser (outline name: **Bull of Zagresh**) alongside the **[[Hydra Goose]]** and **[[FarmPig]]**.

The farm family hid in the **barn basement**; **[[Simon The One Armed Tyrant|Simon]]** and **[[Zara Tideborn]]** murdered the parents to hide the drug. **Children survived.**
