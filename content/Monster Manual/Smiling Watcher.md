# Smiling Watcher

## Visuals

![[Attachments/monsters/smiling-watcher.png|Token]]

**Look:** **Evolved cave-line aberration** — tall, spindly humanoid with pale corded muscle-plate skin; **fixed wide toothy grin**; **no nose**; **horizontal slit "eyes"** pulsing faint red (reads as watching / planning, unlike eyeless **[[Cave Render]]**); deep vertical **chest fissure**; long wiry limbs and elongated claw fingers; often **theatrical upright pose** (arms spread, one leg crossed) — mockery, not a feral crouch.

*Exported once from Foundry actor `iKjeiMthyELT7MBi` (`Windows-sync/Mafia Arc/token_7 (1).png`). Pair with **[[Cave Render]]** token `token_6 (1).png` for the same Nikolai mine line.*

## Role

**[[Dr Nikolai Tesla]] evolution step** above **[[Cave Render]]** (CR 3 **aberration** vs CR ½ **monstrosity**). Same dark-dwelling cannibal breeding program ([wendigo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wendigo)-inspired humans staged as cave survivors), but **more developed**: higher **INT** (10 vs 4), **Mind Glitch** fear, **Snatch** grapple-teleport into darkness, **Unseen Presence** — can **strategize** and ambush rather than pack-rush like base Renders.

Part of the **York Town abandoned mine** distraction (Juniper Islands) alongside feral **Cave Renders** — keep investigators busy while covert **island** work continues (same playbook as the **airship crash** line).

**Table play:** Only **two** **Smiling Watchers** in the counter — **slain** by the **same pair** who cleared the **Cave Renders**: [[Thompson Caneheart]] and [[Matthew -The Jack- Burgess|Jack]]. Session note and transcript **not yet filed**.
