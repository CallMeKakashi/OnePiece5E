# Ashscale Basilisk

## Visuals

![[Attachments/monsters/ashscale-basilisk.png|registry-image]]

**Look:** Oversized scorpion — dark teal-grey chitin with **red hourglass marks** down the segmented back; six small **yellow** eyes; heavy red-tipped pincers and a raised tail stinger. Reads as a giant ash-toned basilisk/scorpion hybrid built for burrowing ambushes.

## Role

**Natural** scorpion-like monstrosities (not Mugen / Nikolai work). They **burrow** under the giant ravine beneath the beaches of **Brook Town** on the **Juniper Islands** archipelago (South Blue). **[[Carrion Vulture|Carrion Vultures]]** and **[[Crystalback Scorpion|Crystalback Scorpions]]** share a deeper, **lightless cave wing** (water channels, man-made bridges, ceiling ambushes).

Ecology:

- **Prey on** anything in their path when hungry.
- **Hunt in packs** when coordinated.
- Burrow to hide from their main predator, the **[[Ravine Scylla]]**.

Use as regional wilderness threats in Juniper ravine / beach encounters (CR 8).
