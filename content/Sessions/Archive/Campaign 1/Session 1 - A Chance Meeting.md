## Blood and Brine - Quest 1 Summary

- **Astor** spoke to an old fisherman about ghost ship sightings at dawn.
- He approached **Ronan** about buying a Devil Fruit.  
- To learn more, they sought out **Linn**, whom a busboy mentioned might have knowledge of Devil Fruits.  

### Encounter with Linn
- **Linn** hesitated to help, knowing Devil Fruits bring trouble.  
- **Pasha**, injured, refused help from Linn.  

### Bounty Hunter Attack
- **Deadeye**, a bounty hunter, recognized Linn.  
- Linn phased the party through the ground into a basement with strange markings that intrigued **Pasha**.  
- They were ambushed by the bounty hunters.  
- The party defeated **Deadeye** and **Iron Grip** easily.  
- **Red Fangs** injected himself with a syringe, transforming into a weakened Sulong-like lion mink.  
- After a tough battle, the party defeated him.  

### Discoveries
- **Pasha** found a **journal** and **three potions** in Red Fangs' bag.  
- Linn revealed he once worked with **Germa 66** and stole a **notebook** that could help Pasha understand his markings and Germa's experiments.  
- **Astor** looted the bounty hunters, finding:  
  - **Berries**  
  - A **dock ticket**  
  - A mysterious **gun** containing **5 colored shots** and **5 black pellets**.  

### Devil Fruit Plan
- Linn suggested they find the **Devil Fruit Encyclopedia** before Ronan buys the fruit.  
- **Ronan**, still injured, rested on the ship.  

### Bounty Posters
The party discovered their new bounties:  
- **Pasha**: **30 million berries** *(Redacted, marked "Alive" due to Germa 66 influence — The Beast Pasha)*  
- **Linn**: **15 million berries** *(The Mad Scientist Linn)*  
- **Astor**: **5 million berries** *(Celeste Astor)*  
- **Ronan**: **5 million berries** *(The Drift Ronan)*  

### Aftermath
- **Germa 66** watched the fight through the bounty hunters, forcing the party to leave the island quickly.  
- Everyone except Linn **leveled up** to lvl2.
