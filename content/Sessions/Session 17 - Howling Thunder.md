## SCENE SETUP — READ ALOUD

> The forest is wrong.
> 
> No birds. No insects.
> 
> The trees ahead are heavy with shapes that sway despite the still air.  
> As you step closer, moonlight reveals what hangs from them —
> 
> **Bodies.**
> 
> Starved. Stripped. Cut open not to kill, but to _see what was inside_.  
> Knives still buried in flesh like markers on a map.
> 
> The ground is soaked with old blood and something sharper — resentment.
> 
> Somewhere in the dark, something breathes…  
> …and smiles.

---

## TREBLE ANTHEM — FLASHBACK BEATS (MID-FIGHT)

Trigger these **between her sniper shots**, not all at once.

### FLASHBACK 1 — _“Sound Is Truth”_

> A younger Treble, ear pressed to a city wall.  
> She hears laughter behind it — and moments later, screaming.
> 
> She doesn’t look away.
> 
> _“If you hear it coming, you deserve to live.”_

### FLASHBACK 2 — _“First Kill”_

> She misses the shot.  
> The target turns — confused, afraid.
> 
> She corrects.
> 
> No guilt. Only precision.

## MECHANICAL NOTES (TREBLE FIGHT)

- Roma **cannot locate her reliably**
    
- Treat forest as **Heavily Obscured**
    
- Treble has **Blindsight (60 ft)** via echolocation
    
- She toys with Roma — this is not tactical necessity

## SCENE SETUP — READ ALOUD

> The arena hums.  
> Not loudly — _constantly_.
> 
> Every step feels heavier than it should.  
> Your thoughts lag half a second behind your body.
> 
> Across from you stands Rhythm Echo — relaxed, smiling.
> 
> “Funny thing about time,” he says.  
> “You only notice it when it’s taken away.”

> Sounds slowed until screams became meaningless vibrations.
> 
> A voice: _“He’s still conscious. Increase it.”_

### FLASHBACK 2 — _“First Mutation Failure”_

> The man convulses.
> 
> Rhythm is forced to watch until the screaming stops.

## RHYTHM ECHO — STAT BLOCK (CR 8)

**Rhythm Echo**  
Medium Humanoid, Chaotic Evil  
**Armor Class:** 17 (Resonant Armor)  
**Hit Points:** 136 (16d8 + 64)  
**Speed:** 30 ft

STR 14 (+2)  
DEX 16 (+3)  
CON 18 (+4)  
INT 12 (+1)  
WIS 14 (+2)  
CHA 10 (+0)

**Saving Throws:** Con +8, Dex +7  
**Skills:** Perception +6, Intimidation +4  
**Resistances:** Thunder; Bludgeoning from nonmagical  
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft  
**Languages:** Common  
**Challenge:** 8 (3,900 XP)

---

### TRAITS

**Vertigo Aura (Recharge 5–6)**  
Creatures within 20 ft must succeed on a **DC 16 CON save** or be affected as by _Slow_ for 1 minute.

**Temporal Displacement**  
Reaction: impose disadvantage on one attack against him.

---

### ACTIONS

**Multiattack.** Two hammer attacks.

**Sonic Hammer.**  
+7 to hit, 1d10 + 4 bludgeoning + 2d6 thunder.

**Pulse Slam (Recharge 6).**  
DC 16 CON save or be knocked prone and stunned until end of next turn.
