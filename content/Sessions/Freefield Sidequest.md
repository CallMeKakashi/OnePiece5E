# SIDEQUEST: FACADE — FULL THROTTLE

---

## SESSION SUMMARY

A morally complex rescue mission disguised as a security job.  
The party arrives at a spice-export island to repair Facade, only to uncover a slave-smuggling operation tied to Alabasta. They must choose between coin, justice, or survival.

Tone: Moral tension, fire-lit night combat, redemption arc spotlight for Kael.

---

# ACT I — THE SIGNAL

While sailing from Anchorpoint to Driftroot:

- Facade’s chassis begins creaking loudly.
    
- Rust flakes fall from joints.
    
- Internal heat fluctuations spike.
    

Facade states:

> “Autorepair protocol requires capsaicin catalyst. High concentration detected ten miles west.”

The signal leads to a spice island: **Freefield**.

---

# FREEFIELD ISLAND

## Overview

A humid agricultural island covered in chili plantations.  
The air burns the lungs slightly from capsaicin in the wind.  
Smoke towers from drying racks.

Public Identity: Prosperous spice exporter.  
Secret Reality: Slave labor imported from Alabasta.

---

# IMPORTANT NPCs

## Bartholomew Spiceveil

Owner of the plantations.

Appearance:

- Saffron-colored silk robes
    
- Gold jewelry shaped like peppers
    
- Always smells faintly of cardamom and smoke
    

Personality:

# ENCOUNTER BALANCE NOTE

Party: 2 Level 5 Monks + 1 Level 5 Paladin

This is now a significantly stronger and highly mobile party with burst damage, Stunning Strike access, Divine Smite nova potential, and strong saving throws.

Recommended Scaling:

## Kael Dhamar (Upgraded)

Increase to Fighter 5 / Rogue 2

- HP: 90 (approx.)
    
- AC: 17 (studded leather + defensive fighting style)
    
- Extra Attack (2) from Fighter 5 (can attack twice with Action, third via offhand bonus action)
    
- Action Surge (1/short rest)
    
- Sneak Attack 2d6
    
- CON 16 with proficiency in Con saves (to resist Stunning Strike)
    

Combat Role:  
Highly mobile duelist. Uses Action Surge early for pressure. Focuses on isolating a monk away from the paladin.

Effective CR: ~5

---

## Cecilia Moore

Keep Rogue 3 but increase survivability slightly.

- HP: 45
    
- AC: 15
    
- Sneak Attack 2d6
    
- Cunning Action every round
    

Effective CR: ~2

---

## Additional Sand Rat Raiders

Add 4–6 Raider Veterans (use Thug or Bandit Captain equivalents depending on desired pressure).

Purpose:

- Prevent monks from freely stun-locking Kael
    
- Create flanking threats
    
- Force the paladin to choose between protection and offense
    

---

## Sharkfin Marauders (If They Join)

Joe Mitch should be CR 5–6 equivalent.  
Crew members CR 2–3 each.

Do NOT combine:

- Full Kael force
    
- AND full Sharkfin force  
    At the same time unless you intend a Deadly+ climactic encounter.
    

Recommended Structure:

- Kael encounter first
    
- Pirates arrive after short rest  
    OR
    
- Pirates intervene mid-fight after Kael retreats
    

---

Encounter Difficulty:  
Kael (CR 5) + Cecilia (CR 2) + 4 Raiders = Hard but fair for Level 5 party.

Adding Pirates makes it Deadly.

---

Design Notes:

- Level 5 monks gain Stunning Strike. Kael needs strong Con saves to avoid being stun-locked.
    
- Paladin burst damage is extremely high at Level 5 (2nd-level smites). Expect nova rounds.
    
- Use terrain: burning fields, difficult terrain, elevation, docks, smoke obscurity.
    
- Allow intelligent retreats and reinforcements to pace difficulty dynamically.

Saving Throws Str +7, Con +7, Wis +4  
Skills Athletics +7, Intimidation +5, Perception +4  
Proficiency Bonus +3

---

### Traits

**Sea Reaver.** Mitch has advantage on attack rolls against creatures he has grappled.

**Relentless (1/Day).** When reduced to 0 HP, Mitch drops to 1 HP instead.

**Brutal Momentum.** When Mitch reduces a creature to 0 HP, he can move up to half his speed and make one additional attack.

---

### Actions

**Multiattack.** Mitch makes three attacks: two with his Cutlass and one with his Harpoon Pistol.

**Sharktooth Cutlass.** Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft.  
Hit: 10 (1d8 + 6) slashing.

**Harpoon Pistol (Reload 1).** Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, range 30/90 ft.  
Hit: 11 (1d10 + 6) piercing.  
If target is Large or smaller, it must succeed on DC 15 Strength save or be grappled (escape DC 15). Mitch can reel grappled targets 10 ft. toward him.

---

### Bonus Action

**Reel In.** Mitch pulls a grappled target 15 ft. closer.
