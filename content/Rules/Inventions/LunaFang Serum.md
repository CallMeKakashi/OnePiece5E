## Lunafang Serum Whitepaper

### Development Process

The **Lunafang Serum** originated as a clandestine weapons program within the **MADS Germa Division**—a secured R&D wing under **Judge Vinsmoke**. When Judge Vinsmoke abandoned the project to pursue artificial-human work, he divested the entire division to the **Marines**. Marine leadership showed little interest; however, **Commodore Briggs** saw the division as a lever for his parallel enterprise, **[[Mugen Industries]]**, and installed **[[Dr Nikolai Tesla]]** as the lead scientist and primary visionary.

On **Driftroot**, **Nyx Velencruz** and **[[Dr Nikolai Tesla]]** continued refinement in the **Old City** (overseen by a hired **[[Berserker]]** until a crazed **[[Saber-Toothed Tiger]]** test subject killed him). Aiming to create enhanced combatants, the project relied heavily on unethical human experimentation. Among the subjects, one stood out—**Subject-0**, referred to as **Zero** in internal documentation. Zero, a Mink, exhibited remarkable resilience and adaptability, which intrigued the scientists and led to the further refinement of the serum.

The serum’s foundational premise involved harnessing dormant genetic traits and inducing temporary states of heightened physical power. The research team, under the command of key Germa scientists, employed chemical catalysts to manipulate the Mink's natural Sulong transformation.

### Key Phases of Development

1. **Genetic Sequencing**: Subject-0's genetic profile was analyzed to identify the markers responsible for Sulong activation. Researchers extracted and synthesized key proteins believed to trigger the transformation.
    
2. **Catalyst Formulation**: Using extracted compounds, the team created a volatile serum that would mimic the effects of a full moon, bypassing the natural lunar requirement.
    
3. **Trial and Error**: Subject-0 underwent numerous trials, with early versions of the serum causing severe neurological distress. Despite setbacks, the serum was iteratively improved, stabilizing its effects for short durations.
    
4. **Behavioral Control**: A secondary compound was introduced to heighten aggression and suppress reasoning, making the subject more effective in combat. This led to the violent and uncontrollable state witnessed in other test subjects such as **Red Fangs**.
    

### Mechanism of Action

The Lunafang Serum operates by directly stimulating the subject’s adrenal and nervous systems. It triggers a surge of hormones that amplify physical strength, speed, and resilience. For Mink subjects, it forcibly activates the Sulong state, bypassing the need for moonlight exposure.

In non-Mink users, the serum temporarily enhances combat abilities, although the side effects are far more severe. The substance binds to neural receptors, flooding the brain with aggressive impulses, which are nearly impossible to suppress without external intervention.

### Effects and Side Effects

**Primary Effects:**

- Enhanced strength, agility, and stamina
    
- Heightened sensory perception
    
- Temporary regenerative abilities
    

**Side Effects:**

- Severe muscle and nerve fatigue
    
- Psychological instability
    
- Aggressive, uncontrollable behavior
    
- Risk of permanent neural damage
    

Prolonged use has shown fatal effects in non-Mink subjects, and even Minks face severe health deterioration over time.

### Application and Dosage

The serum was administered through reinforced syringes designed to withstand the violent reactions it induced. Typical dosage ranged from **10ml to 25ml**, with higher doses exponentially increasing both effects and side effects.

In emergency situations, subjects were injected directly into major muscle groups to ensure rapid absorption. Continuous use was strictly avoided due to the cumulative risks.

### Ethical Implications

The Lunafang Serum stands as a grim reminder of Germa 66’s willingness to pursue power at any cost. Subject-0 (referred to as **Zero** in internal documentation) endured extensive suffering under the guise of scientific advancement. While his resilience allowed him to survive, countless others perished in the name of progress.

### Future Implications

Red Fangs stole the majority of working samples from a research facility. Additionally, Subject-0 has gone missing after a breakout caused by a strange awakening. He now goes by the name **[[Pasha]]** and is currently free unto the world.
