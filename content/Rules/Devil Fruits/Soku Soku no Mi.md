## **Phase-Phase Fruit - Abilities and Techniques**

### 🌀 **Core Power**

- Allows Linn to **become intangible** and pass through **solid matter** (walls, floors, objects, or even attacks).
- He can **control his own tangibility**, selectively phasing only parts of his body.
- Unlike the Clear-Clear Fruit, this ability doesn’t grant invisibility. Instead, he **blurs** slightly when phasing, like a mirage.

---

### ⚙️ **Combat Applications**

1. **Phantom Step**
    
    - Instantly **phase through the floor or walls** to reposition.
    - Can be used mid-combat for **evasive maneuvers** or sneak attacks.
2. **Ghost Palm**
    
    - Phases his hand through an opponent’s body, grabbing or crushing internal organs.
    - Causes **intense pain** without external damage.
    - This is difficult to counter without Haki.
3. **Phase Recoil**
    
    - Allows him to **phase through an attack** and counter mid-motion.
    - Especially useful against **weapons or projectile attacks**.
4. **Echo Phase**
    
    - Briefly leaves an **afterimage** of himself by phasing multiple times in succession.
    - Confuses enemies with unpredictable movements.
5. **Phase Cage**
    
    - Phases **through the ground** and partially solidifies to trap an opponent.
    - Often used for capturing targets rather than direct combat.

---

### 🧠 **Utility and Support**

1. **Silent Passage**
    
    - Moves **through walls** silently, making him an exceptional infiltrator.
2. **Underground Extraction**
    
    - Can phase allies through solid floors or walls to escape danger.
    - Useful during stealth missions.
3. **Object Extraction**
    
    - Phases objects or weapons **out of locked containers**.
    - Limited to non-living objects.
4. **Phase Lock**
    
    - Temporarily leaves a part of an object inside a solid wall.
    - Can be used to **disarm enemies** by phasing weapons into walls.

---

### ❗ **Weaknesses**

- **Haki**: Physical attacks imbued with Haki will **bypass** Linn's phasing, directly striking him.
- **Seastone**: Completely nullifies his Devil Fruit powers.
- **Limited Phasing Time**: Continuous phasing drains stamina quickly, forcing him to use it strategically.
- **Environmental Hazard**: Phasing through hazardous substances like seawater or molten metal is risky.

---

### 🌪️ **Potential Advanced Techniques (If Awakened)**

- **Spatial Distortion**: Can manipulate the density of walls or floors, phasing parts of them to create small passageways.
- **Phantom Echo**: Creates temporary “echoes” of his phased state, projecting illusions to disorient enemies.
- **Phase Collapse**: Forces objects to phase back into a solid state **inside each other**, causing destructive ruptures.
- **Dimensional Fold**: Briefly folds space around him, using it to **redirect attacks** or reposition faster.
