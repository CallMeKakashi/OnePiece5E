# Days 6–7 — Driftroot to South Blue

## Setting

- **When:** Marine brig incident through departure from [[Driftroot Isle]]
- **Where:** Marine vessel, [[Driftroot Isle]] (Egg Bank, docks, Old City aftermath), open sea
- **Author:** Ship chronicle (third person; compiled after the fact)

## Entry

### Marine vessel — the brig

The Marine ship was never meant to be quiet, but that night the hull creaked almost respectfully—as if it knew it carried something that should not be disturbed.

Marines cornered a small monkey on the deck ropes and mocked him as a pirate's pet. Lantern light shifted; a shadow swallowed it. One step, then **boom**—one Marine launched into the brig bars, the second skidding across the deck before [[⚔️ Fenris Wolfblood — “The Blood of Sun and Shadow”|Fenris]] stepped between them and the animal. Nine feet of scarred muscle; blood on his knuckles; voice low: *Don't pick on my friend.*

They brought precision, not numbers. Steel flashed. Fenris went down hard—ribs screaming, mouth full of blood. **Vex** shrieked; the monkey bit the injector on Fenris's sealed locket and drove it into his chest. A Marine with an immaculate rapier spoke of "pure Justice" and raised his blade.

Something inhaled behind him. Wood creaked. Iron groaned. Fenris rose—bigger, broader—crimson fluid surging under his tattoos, eyes igniting scarlet. The Marine ran. **Drez** was already gone and never saw what he left behind.

### Driftroot Isle — mist and Leon

[[Driftroot Isle]] burned when the crew arrived—smoke in the canopy, bells in the east, silvery mist along the rooftops. **Commander Leon** faced a hulking silhouette in the street. **Shako** read the fight before it began; Fenris stepped between Leon and the crew with a growl: *Don't touch my crew.*

**[[🗡️ Goru Yamashita (formerly Goru Valencruz)|Goru]]** recognized Leon from the orphanage years and struck him for poisoning the island—for the mist, the rivers, for **Laziel** and officer Drez. Leon swore he was confined to the ship, that he'd been told it was aid. **Nyx** called it training blind. The crew demanded proof, not excuses.

### Egg Bank — the cure

Nyx led them through passages beneath Egg Bank. **Facade** rewired dispersal mechanisms with clipped precision—this would aerosolize the cure; western docks might be spared; nobles would breathe the same air. **Malik** said do it. Screams softened into breath. **Flint** and **Robb** convulsed, then lived.

### Fenris and the crew

Near the docks Fenris scrubbed dried blood from his arms, red still pulsing beneath the ink. Shako asked if he'd always been able to transform; Fenris said he hoped he never would. Kalla had seen his eyes. Fenris answered: *I know where you are. I hear you breathe.* Vex climbed to his shoulder. *I won't hurt you—not any of you,* he said. Facade called that statistically reassuring.

### Sana and Leon

At the clinic **Sana** froze; Leon stopped. Five years collapsed into an embrace while Nyx watched, jaw tight—orphans before the fire; everyone chose something after.

### Naming the crew

That night nobles and commoners shared fires. Flint and Robb sat among them, astonished to survive poisoning and to see nobles share food.

Later the table argued names. Fenris thought they were simply Blackhand. **Sana** offered **Gentle Giant Pirates**. Fenris laughed—startled, genuine—*They always feared me. Never bothered learning my name.* Shako said that changes now.

### Departure

Leon left at dawn to hunt Drez, saluting like a man, not a Marine. Goru took the title if the people would have him; Fenris said protect them. Nyx stayed silent and stayed.

When [[Dravos]] grinned about a **Thousand Mile Tunnel**, Malik refused on principle; Shako insisted physics was crying. The **Rustwing** screamed up the Red Line and fell into the South Blue with the crew laughing and screaming alive.

The **[[Gentle Giant Pirates]]** were born—not from fear, but from choosing each other anyway.
