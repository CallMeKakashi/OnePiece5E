## Crafting

Crafting items is a key part of a majority of the roles aboard the crew, particularly Artificers, Shipwrights, Blacksmiths, and Doctors. Crafting various items such as Medicines (Spell Scrolls and Potions), Armor, Weapons, and other forms of utility.
There are several requirements for the crafting to be initiated: experience, tools, materials, and the time to craft. In addition, crafting items does require the input of the DM.
