# Gentle Giant Pirates

The **Gentle Giant Pirates** are a unit of the [[Blackhand]] crew. A small but capable squad, they bring scholarly insight and master craftsmanship to the Blackhand fleet.

- [[Kalla of Shandia]] — scholar, linguist, and puzzle-solver; Skypiean from the White Sea
