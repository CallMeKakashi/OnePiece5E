## Description

An old fishman, scarred and aged. His body tells the story of a lifetime spent fighting — every mark a testament to endurance.

## Role

[[Blackhand]] crew member and combat veteran. Trained [[Ronan]] in Fishman Karate, believing that true strength came from enduring pain.

## Personal Quests

- Protecting the crew he called family

## Backstory

Morley served as one of the seasoned fighters in [[Blackhand Cane]]'s crew. He was present during the Germa facility raid that freed [[Baptiste]], fighting alongside [[Renzo]], [[Tray]], and the rest.

He took [[Ronan]] under his wing and trained him in Fishman Karate, instilling the philosophy that strength is forged through suffering and survival.

Morley sacrificed himself protecting Ronan from **Marine Captain Briggs** — he was shot, and then killed by **Lieutenant Voss**, who punched through his chest. His death remains a defining moment in Ronan's story.
