## Description

Recent recruit of the [[Braveheart Pirates]] — an **undead cyborg** artificer shell animated and directed by **OHM**, an onboard AI. Outwardly Façade; inwardly machine intelligence plus necrotech augmentations.

## Role

Crew member (**[[Malak Samum]]** secured the unit as "Operation: Facade"). Status: headed to South Blue.

**OHM** runs the main body; a **[[Homunculus Servant]]** homunculus acts as OHM's **remote extension** — a **light spider** drone that can **fly** (web-spider style) and handle **autonomous tasks** while Facade is elsewhere.
