## Description

Swordsman of exceptional speed and aerial capability.

## Role

Founding member of the [[Motley Crew]]. Mentored by [[Blackhand|Blackhand Cane]].

## Personal Quests

(Unknown — to be developed)

## Backstory

Trained under Blackhand Cane, developing flying slash techniques and sky-walking ability.

## Abilities

### Flying Slash

_Prerequisite: level 8_

With intense training your weapons can extend their reach. Your weapons that normally have a 5ft. reach gain a reach of 30ft. Half the normal damage of this attack until level 10.

### Sky Walk

_Prerequisite: Either Dexterity 18 OR a walking speed greater than 40 AND level 8_

Your character gains a flying speed equal to your walking speed. If your character is Restrained or Incapacitated you will begin to fall.
