Revolutionary group from [[Decibella Kingdom]] fighting against the [[Soundless 5]]'s oppressive silence regime.

## Members

- [[Cadence]] — rebellion base leader
- [[Riff Sin]] — musician and rebel fighter

## Overview

The Decibella Revolutionary is a resistance movement operating within [[Decibella Kingdom]], opposing the enforced silence imposed by the ruling [[Soundless 5]]. Members operate from hidden bases in the slums, using music and sound as acts of defiance. Their fight is existential — sound itself is rebellion.
