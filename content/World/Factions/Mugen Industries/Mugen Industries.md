Industrial/corporate faction connected to the G-45 facility, chimeras, and cogwork technology.

## Members

- [[Hallow]] — antagonist at the G-45 facility

## Overview

Mugen Industries operates industrial facilities including the G-45 site where chimeras were created and a cogwork core was maintained. Their operations were encountered during the [[Sessions/Sidequests/Spider's Nest]] sidequest, culminating in the destruction of the facility.
