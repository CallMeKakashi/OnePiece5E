### Specialist Path

At 3rd level, you choose the type of specialist you are, your area of study if you will. Your choice grants you features at 5th level and again at 9th and 15th level. Your Specialist Paths include the defensive Armorer, the robotically assisted Battlesmith, the bombastic Artillerist, the clever Alchemist, the ever-potent Elementalist, and the stalwart Safeguard.
