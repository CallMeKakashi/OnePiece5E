## Role

**Lunafang-mutated farm pig** from the **West Town** fertilizer incident (same feed poisoning as **[[FarmBull]]** and **[[Hydra Goose]]**). Grew into a spined, relentless boar-like predator (**Spine Burst**, **Relentless** — drops to 1 HP once per day).

Part of Nikolai's **Lunafang** pipeline on **Juniper**; Simon and Zara's cover-up killings apply to the whole farm scene.
