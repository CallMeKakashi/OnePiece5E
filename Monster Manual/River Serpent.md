# River Serpent

## Visuals

![[Attachments/monsters/river-serpent.png|registry-image]]

**Look:** **Massive hooded serpent** — slate-blue scales, cream banded underbelly, cobra-like hood ringed with **bone ribs ending in pale digging claws** (built to tear through cave walls); small **glowing yellow** eyes; chin barbels; white head spines. Large enough to **engulf a human** whole when it strikes. (Foundry token art is mislabeled "SCYLLA" — vault title is **River Serpent**, distinct from **[[Ravine Scylla]]**.)

*Exported once from Foundry actor `sSr9R6GengZ7gS3h` (`Windows-sync/Mafia Arc/token_4 (3).png`). Stats sync separately.*

## Role

**Natural** apex serpent of the **Brook Town ravine cave system** (**Juniper Islands**) — burrows through wet stone and river channels alongside the **[[Ravine Scylla|Ravine Scyllas]]**, but **losing the territory war**. Scyllas have expanded; **only a few River Serpents remain**. **CR 9**, **darkvision-minded** (glowing eyes), and **more intelligent** than most cave fauna (basilisks, lampreys, crystalbacks) — patient ambusher, not a mindless swarm beast.

**Currently hibernating** and laying low in deeper wall-burrows until the cycle turns or prey blunders into its lair. When active: **aggressive**, uses **Whirlpool Rush** in flooded tunnels and its bulk to pin or swallow prey.
