# Puppy

## Role

**Lunafang-mutated family pet** (CR 3) from the **West Town** farm — the household dog exposed to the same faulty **[[LunaFang Serum|Lunafang]]**-laced fertilizer in the feed as **[[FarmBull]]**, **[[FarmPig]]**, and **[[Hydra Goose]]**. Livestock turned first; the dog followed. Foundry text still calls it a "wolf" in trait lines (**Pack Tactics**, **Many Eyes**, **Rend Flesh**); treat it as the family's corrupted pet, not a wild pack animal.

Part of the Juniper **West Town** horror beat: survivors barricaded in the barn while mutates roamed the yard; **[[Simon The One Armed Tyrant|Simon]]** and **[[Zara Tideborn]]** killed the parents to bury the drug trail.
