# Serum Enforcer — “Permanently Changed”

## Visuals

![[Attachments/monsters/aberrant-abomination.png|registry-image]]

**Look:** Swollen, scarred brute — **overdosed** muscle growth, warped proportions, and heat-scorched skin where the serum keeps “burning.”

## Role

Lumafang overuse mutation; ring enforcer / mini-boss creature.

## Stat block

_Large Monstrosity, Chaotic Evil, CR 6_

**AC** 16 **HP** 136 (16d10 + 48) **Speed** 40 ft.  
STR 20 (+5) DEX 14 (+2) CON 16 (+3) INT 6 WIS 10 CHA 8  
**Saves** Str +8, Con +6 **Resistances** Fire, Non-magical Bludgeoning/Slashing  
**Condition Immunities** Frightened  
**Senses** Darkvision 60 ft. PP 10

**Multiattack.** 2 Claws + 1 Bite.

- **Claw.** +8 to hit, 10 ft., 14 (2d8 + 5) slashing + 1d6 fire.
- **Bite.** +8 to hit, 5 ft., 17 (2d10 + 5) piercing.

**Raging Mutagen.** Regain 10 HP at start of turn unless took fire/acid damage previous round.  
**Blood Frenzy.** Advantage on attacks vs creatures below half HP.
