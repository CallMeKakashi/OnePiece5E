# Stitched Horror

## Visuals

![[Attachments/monsters/stitched-horror.png|Token]]

**Look:** **Man–pig patchwork bruiser** — emaciated pale pink-grey humanoid frame with **porcine snout**, small sunken eyes, upright pig ears, and jagged teeth; coarse dark **bristle mane** down the spine; crude **cross-stitches** and seam lines (X on the back, neck/jaw sutures) where flesh was sewn together. Low **rush crouch** — hyperviolent charger, not a skulker.

*Exported once from Foundry actor `tsQw96LDgD9Rm8MZ` (`Windows-sync/Mafia Arc/token_3 (1).png`). Generic portrait for any **Stitched Horror** from the airship crash — not a named NPC.*

## Role

**Nikolai / Mugen airship experiment** (CR 5) — forced **human + pig** fusion from the same aberrant line as the **[[Aberrant Abomination]]**, **[[Carrion Stalker|Carrion Stalkers]]**, and **[[Red Maw Beast|Red Maw Beasts]]**. **Patchwork Flesh** and **Aberrant Regeneration** match the other crash mutates. Forensics would still read human DNA in the mix.

**Combat behavior:** **Hyperviolent** — **charges** straight at enemies and tries to **crush** them with heavy **Slam** blows (STR 18 bruiser), not pack tactics or ambush play.

Deployed from the **crashed airship** as part of Nikolai's **distraction** while covert **island** work continued (**[[Cave Render]]** mine plot).
