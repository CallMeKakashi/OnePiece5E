## Role

**Natural** aberrations in the **underground ravine waterways** beneath **Brook Town** (**Juniper Islands**) — same cave system as **[[Carrion Vulture|Carrion Vultures]]** and **[[Crystalback Scorpion|Crystalback Scorpions]]**.

Demon-faced, **bloodthirsty** predators with almost no **survival instinct** beyond feeding. They **latch** onto prey (**Latch Bite**), then **Blood Drain** on grappled targets. Preyed on by vultures and scorpions when humans are scarce.
