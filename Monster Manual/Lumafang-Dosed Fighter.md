# Lumafang-Dosed Fighter

## Visuals

![[Attachments/monsters/berserker.webp|registry-image]]

**Look:** A ring bruiser with **glowing veins** and twitchy movements — fighting on borrowed time from a volatile injection.

## Role

Ring test subject pumped full of serum; burns out fast and explodes on death.

## Stat block

_Medium Humanoid (Any), Chaotic Evil, CR 3_

**AC** 15 (unarmored defense via serum toughening) **HP** 60 (8d8 + 24) **Speed** 40 ft.  
STR 18 (+4) DEX 14 (+2) CON 16 (+3) INT 8 WIS 10 CHA 8  
**Saves** Str +6, Con +5 **Skills** Athletics +6  
**Resistances** Fire, Psychic (for 1 minute after dose)  
**Multiattack.** 2 Feral Punches.  
**Feral Punch.** +6 to hit, 5 ft., 9 (1d10 + 4) bludgeoning + 1d6 fire.  
**Unstable Serum.** At end of each turn, take 5 (1d10) damage; on death, explode 10 ft., DC 13 Dex save or 7 (2d6) fire.
