# Swarm of Quippers

## Visuals

![[Attachments/monsters/swarm-of-quippers.webp|registry-image]]

**Look:** Stock **quipper swarm** — dense school of tiny piranha-like fish, silver flash and needle teeth when the mass surges; reads as one writhing medium token. In **Puff-Island** lung tunnels, reskin as **Sneezefish** (wall-embedded fish that inflate and blast air).

*Exported once from Foundry actor `pcZNIR7tNoqOt3mq` (`systems/dnd5e/tokens/beast/SwarmQuippers.webp` — stock token). Generic underwater swarm filler; table instance below was reskinned for an astral trial.*

## Role

**Generic SRD swarm** (CR 1) — **Blood Frenzy**, **Swarm** occupancy, underwater **Bites** (piercing). Filler for any shoal, river run, or flooded dungeon — **not a named NPC**.

**Table reskin — Sneezefish Swarm:** **[[Kaen Solaris]]** astral trial **[[Sessions/Sidequests/THE ISLAND THAT BREATHES|The Island That Breathes]]** (Puff-Island lung tunnels) assigned **[[Malphas]]** and **[[Shako]]**. Use stock stats but swap damage to **bludgeoning** and add **Air Burst** (push) per sidequest prep — fish blow PCs into pit depressions rather than blood-frenzy to the death.

**Behavior at the table:** Swarm **flees once enough were killed** — sidequest tactics: **never fights to the death**; **scatters at 10 HP or less** (same “break morale and run” beat).
