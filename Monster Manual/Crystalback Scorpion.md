# Crystalback Scorpion

## Visuals

![[Attachments/monsters/crystalback-scorpion.png|registry-image]]

**Look:** Low-slung **crystal-armored scorpion** — indigo carapace with glowing **cyan hexagonal** plates on claws, shoulders, and tail blade; orange slit eyes and a toothy under-mouth. Built for ceiling ambushes in dark caves (matches **Crystal Burst** throwing spines).

## Role

**Natural** cave predators (not Nikolai work). **Coexist with [[Carrion Vulture|Carrion Vultures]]** in the **lightless Brook Town ravine caves** (**Juniper Islands**): cling to **ceilings**, **light-blue glowing** crystal carapaces lure prey, then **drop in packs**.

**Back crystals** are armored and used as **throwing scythes** — impale targets in a wide burst (**Crystal Burst**), then **regrow** quickly. Most of their lives are spent in the dark, **sedimenting** and **growing** new crystal growths on the walls.

When **human prey** is scarce, they hunt **[[Lamprey Horror|Lamprey Horrors]]** in the adjoining streams, using **venom** for easy kills.
