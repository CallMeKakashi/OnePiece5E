### **Standard Healing Kit**
- **Healing Effect:** A single use of a health kit restores **2d4 + 2 HP**.
- **Application:** It takes **1 action** to apply the kit to yourself or another creature within reach.
- **Stabilization:** If a character is at **0 HP** and dying, the kit can be used to stabilize them without requiring a Medicine check.
### **Enhanced or Special Health Kits**

You can add variety with unique health kits found throughout the Grand Line: 
- **Enhanced Medical Kit:** Heals **4d4 + 4 HP**.
- **Doctor's Medical Kit:** Heals **8d4 + 8 HP**.
- **Deluxe Medical Kit:** Heals **10d4 + 20 HP**.
