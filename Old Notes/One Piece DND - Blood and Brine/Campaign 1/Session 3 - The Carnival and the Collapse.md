### 🧭 **Session 3 TL;DR – "The Carnival, the Collapse, and the Compass"**

**Setting:** Craw Island – a notorious black market hub in the North Blue.

---

### 🚢 **Arrival & New Threat**

- The crew arrives on **Craw Island**, looking for supplies.
    
- As they dock, **Dr. Nikolai** (a Germa scientist) contacts them with a **threatening call**, proving they're being watched.
    
- **Pasha befriends a dock kid** with chocolate and leaves the ship under his care while they explore the island.
    

---

### 🎪 **Grinjaw’s Pirate Festival**

- The crew discovers a loud, rowdy **festival hosted by the Grinjaw Pirates**, who are boasting about defeating **Red Fangs**.
    
- They compete in the games:
    
    - **Win the first two rounds easily**.
        
    - In the **final event**, they’re moments from victory when **Grinjaw’s ship is suddenly torn apart**, as if **devoured by something immense**.
        
- **Grinjaw panics and tries to escape**, but:
    
    - **Ronan** grabs him, **steals his pistol**, and **extorts the promised 5 million berries**.
        
    - He fires a **final bullet into Grinjaw’s ruined ship** before leaving triumphantly.
        

---

### ⚔️ **Ambush by Vice Admiral Horus**

- As the crew tries to flee, they are ambushed by the **massive and terrifying Vice Admiral Horus**.
    
- Despite resisting as best they can, **Horus easily defeats them**.
    
- **Pasha uses one of his two serums** to try and gain the upper hand—but it's not enough.
    

---

### 🌀 **A Mysterious Rescue**

- The crew awakens, defeated—but alive.
    
- Surprisingly, they're **rescued and watched over by Veyl and Serica**, elite members of **Sixfold**, sent on orders from their leader.
    
- Their only instruction: **"Take the crew wherever they want to go."**
    
    - The reason behind this mysterious protection remains unknown.
        

---

### 🧭 **The Compass Speaks**

- **Astor checks his compass**—it begins pointing directly at the **Reverse Mountain**.
    
- The message is clear:  
    **Their journey now leads to the Grand Line.**
