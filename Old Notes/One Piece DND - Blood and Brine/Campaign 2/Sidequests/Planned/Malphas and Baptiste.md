# **SIDEQUEST: “THE SHADOWS’ PRICE”**

### _A One-Piece–style Level 3 Sidequest for Baptiste & Malphas_

---

## **MAIN PREMISE**

Malphas has been **captured** by a secret bounty-hunting duo known only as **The Shadows**. They intend to **auction him** at an underground slave/devil-fruit/weapon auction run by a minor underworld broker.

Baptiste, following rumors, must infiltrate the auction to rescue Malphas — while Malphas tries to escape from his captors… OR aid Baptiste from inside.

Everything is framed in One Piece’s exaggerated, chaotic energy.

# **ACT I — Baptiste’s Investigation: “Where is Malphas?”**

### **Hooks**

- Someone saw **a chained man with horns** dragged into the old spice warehouse district.
    
- Rumors of a “**special lot**” in tonight’s Black Auction.
    

### **Scene Options**

#### **1. Street Rough-Up (RP + Info Gathering)**

Baptiste intimidates/charms local smugglers.

**DCs**

- DC 13 Persuasion → They reveal an auction happening tonight.
    
- DC 15 Intimidation → They give the password too.
    

**Password:**  
“_Salt burns deeper than steel._”

### **Steps**

1. **Entry Checkpoint**
    
    - A guard checks the password.
        
2. **Malphas’ POV**
    
    - He is chained in a backstage cage.
        
    - He can make rolls to slip information to Baptiste (whisper to an attendant, drop a clue, break something to cause a distraction).
        
3. **Auction Chaos**
    
    - Other lots: rare seafood beasts, illegal weapons, captured Zoan-user (fake).
        
    - When Malphas is brought on stage, the Shadow Duo appears to “inspect the merchandise.”
        

### **Skill Challenge (Before the fight)**

To get into position before combat:

- Stealth / Acrobatics / Deception DC 14  
    Success: Baptiste gets advantage on his first round attack  
    Failure: The Shadows call out:  
    “**We know you’re here, stalker!**”

# **ACT III — Boss Fight: The Shadows**

When Baptiste reveals himself OR Malphas breaks free, the Shadows drop the act and attack.

Other bidders flee → arena becomes an open battlefield.

# **STAT BLOCKS**

These are balanced for **Lv 3 PCs** (two-player party). They are dangerous but fair — built like One Piece mid-level minibosses.

---

## **KARESH “THE WHISPER”**

_Medium Humanoid (CR 3 Elite)_

**HP:** 55  
**AC:** 15 (Studded Leather)  
**Speed:** 35 ft

**STR 10 | DEX 16 | CON 14 | INT 12 | WIS 14 | CHA 12**

**Saving Throws:** Dex +5, Wis +4  
**Skills:** Stealth +6, Acrobatics +5, Perception +4  
**Traits:**

- **Shadow Step (Recharge 5–6):** Teleports up to 20 ft to dim light. Next attack has advantage.
    
- **Chain Snare:** On hit, may force DC 13 STR save or restrain target.
    

**ACTIONS**

**Multiattack.** Two chain strikes.

**Shadow Chain**  
+5 to hit | 1d6+3 slashing  
If both hits land in same turn → target restrained (no save).

**Garrote (Special)**  
If behind a restrained creature →  
2d6+3 slashing; target must make DC 13 Con save or fall unconscious 1 round (non-lethal).

---

## **MURA “THE GRIN”**

_Medium Humanoid (CR 3 Elite)_

**HP:** 65  
**AC:** 16 (Reinforced Plating)  
**Speed:** 30 ft

**STR 16 | DEX 12 | CON 16 | INT 10 | WIS 12 | CHA 14**

**Saving Throws:** Con +5  
**Skills:** Athletics +5, Intimidation +4

**Traits:**

- **Killing Intent (Minor Haki):** Creatures within 10 ft must make DC 12 Wis save or have disadvantage on first attack against him.
    
- **Brutal Grip:** He has advantage on grapples.
    

**ACTIONS**

**Multiattack.** 2 cleaver swings.

**Cleave**  
+5 to hit | 1d10+3 slashing  
On a hit → target must succeed STR save DC 13 or be knocked prone.

**Headbutt (Bonus Action, 1/turn)**  
1d6+3 bludgeoning; target Con save DC 12 or be stunned until next turn.

---

# **Malphas’ Interaction During Fight**

He is chained **but can break free**:

**Break DC:** Athletics 14 or he can use any supernatural feature you gave him (infernal strength, bound magic spark, etc.).

Once free, Malphas can:

- Join combat
    
- Tackle one of the Shadows
    
- Cause environmental hazards (dropping auction crates, releasing animals)
    

---

# **ENVIRONMENT HAZARDS**

Use One Piece chaos:

### **Loose Sea Beasts**

- A mini sea-cow calf bursts out!
    
- It may attack randomly or panic and smash platforms.
    

### **Exploding Spice Barrels**

Throwing/shooting them creates **smoke clouds** and **1d6 fire damage** in 5 ft.

### **Collapsing Stage**

Dex save DC 13 → avoid falling prone.

# **ENDING OPTIONS**

### **1. Capture a Shadow**

Interrogate them later. They reveal:

- They were hired by a mysterious buyer.
    
- The buyer wanted Malphas alive because of **some trait Malphas has** (you can reveal this later).
    

### **2. Kill Them**

Rumors spread: “Two masked bounty hunters died at a black auction.”  
A bigger criminal group takes interest.

### **3. They Escape**

Very One Piece.  
They vow:  
“Next time we take both your heads.”

### **4. Malphas Is Traumatized or Empowered**

You decide his growth arc.
