Here are the stat blocks for the key NPCs involved in **Episode 6: "Thorn in the Road."** I have used official D&D 5e stat blocks as the chassis and applied _One Piece_ re-flavoring to fit the narrative.

### ⚔️ The Brothers (South City Encounter)

#### **Goru (The Exhausted Swordsman)**

_Base: **Veteran** (CR 3)_

- **Visual:** A samurai-style swordsman in tattered robes. He is heavily bandaged and bleeding (starts encounter at **half HP** if the party arrives late).
    
- **AC:** 17 (Splint) | **HP:** 58 (9d8 + 18) | **Speed:** 30 ft.
    
- **STR:** 16 (+3) | **DEX:** 13 (+1) | **CON:** 14 (+2)
    
- **Actions:**
    
    - **Multiattack:** Makes two Longsword attacks and one Shortsword attack.
        
    - **Longsword:** +5 to hit, 1d8+3 slashing.
        
    - **Shortsword:** +5 to hit, 1d6+3 piercing.
        
- **Reaction - Parry:** Adds +2 to AC against one melee attack.
    

#### **Nyx (The Rival Brother)**

_Base: **Gladiator** (CR 5) - Slightly nerfed for an ally/rival role._

- **Visual:** Wears sleek, dark leather armor. Wields a massive sword-spear hybrid. Arrogant smirk.
    
- **AC:** 16 (Studded Leather + Shield) | **HP:** 112 (15d8 + 45) | **Speed:** 30 ft.
    
- **STR:** 18 (+4) | **DEX:** 15 (+2) | **CON:** 16 (+3)
    
- **Feature - Brute:** Melee weapon deals one extra die of damage (included below).
    
- **Actions:**
    
    - **Multiattack:** Makes three melee attacks.
        
    - **Shockwave Spear (Spear):** +7 to hit, 2d6+4 piercing.
        
    - **Shield Bash:** +7 to hit, 2d4+4 bludgeoning. If target is Medium or smaller, it must succeed on a DC 15 STR save or be knocked prone.
        
- **Reaction - Parry:** Adds +3 to AC against one melee attack.

### 👹 The Tragic Bandits (Old City Encounter)

#### **Rob-Chimera (The Beetle Brawler)**

_Base: **Hook Horror** (CR 3)_

- **Visual:** Rob’s upper body is swollen with a black, shiny carapace. His left arm is a massive pincer claw. His human face is stretched across the beetle's chest, weeping.
    
- **AC:** 15 (Natural Armor) | **HP:** 75 (10d10 + 20) | **Speed:** 30 ft., Climb 30 ft.
    
- **Senses:** Blindsight 60 ft. (Echolocation)
    
- **Traits:**
    
    - **Flickering Mind:** If Rob hears his name, he must make a **DC 14 Wisdom Save**. On a failure, he attacks normally. On a success, he screams in confusion and has **Disadvantage** on attacks for that turn.
        
- **Actions:**
    
    - **Multiattack:** Two Hook attacks.
        
    - **Crushing Pincer (Hook):** +6 to hit, reach 10 ft. _Hit:_ 11 (2d6 + 4) piercing damage.
        
        - _Grapple:_ On a hit, the target is Grappled (Escape DC 14).
            

#### **Flint-Chimera (The Quill Gunner)**

_Base: **Manticore** (CR 3) - **Flight Removed**._

- **Visual:** Flint is hunched over on all fours like a lizard, but his back is covered in long, needle-sharp quills. His eyes are yellow and reptilian.
    
- **AC:** 14 (Natural Armor) | **HP:** 68 (9d10 + 18) | **Speed:** 40 ft.
    
- **Traits:**
    
    - **Flickering Mind:** Same as Rob. If the party appeals to his humanity, he might hesitate.
        
- **Actions:**
    
    - **Multiattack:** Three attacks: one bite and two claws, OR three tail spikes.
        
    - **Bite:** +5 to hit, 1d8+3 piercing.
        
    - **Claw:** +5 to hit, 1d6+3 slashing.
        
    - **Tail Spike (Ranged):** +5 to hit, range 100/200 ft. _Hit:_ 7 (1d8 + 3) piercing damage.

### ⚓ The Dispensary Guards (Eggbank Encounter)

#### **Marine Captain (The Dispensary Warden)**

_Base: **Knight** (CR 3)_

- **Visual:** Wears a heavy "Justice" coat and a gas mask (or Hazmat cowl). Wields a Greatsword.
    
- **AC:** 18 (Plate) | **HP:** 52 (8d8 + 16) | **Speed:** 30 ft.
    
- **Traits:**
    
    - **Leadership (Recharges after Short/Long Rest):** For 1 minute, the Captain can utter a command whenever a nonhostile creature within 30 ft makes an attack roll or save. The creature can add a **d4** to its roll.
        
- **Actions:**
    
    - **Multiattack:** Two Greatsword attacks.
        
    - **Greatsword:** +5 to hit, 2d6+3 slashing.
        

#### **Marine Rifleman (Standard Guard)**

_Base: **Scout** (CR 1/2) - Reflavored with Musket._

- **AC:** 13 (Leather) | **HP:** 16 (3d8 + 3) | **Speed:** 30 ft.
    
- **Actions:**
    
    - **Multiattack:** Two attacks.
        
    - **Musket (Longbow stats):** +4 to hit, range 150/600 ft. _Hit:_ 1d8+2 piercing.
        
    - **Bayonet (Shortsword):** +4 to hit, 1d6+2 piercing.
        

#### **Marine Enforcer (Heavy Guard)**

_Base: **Thug** (CR 1/2)_

- **AC:** 11 (Leather) | **HP:** 32 (5d8 + 10) | **Speed:** 30 ft.
    
- **Traits:**
    
    - **Pack Tactics:** Advantage on attacks if an ally is within 5 ft of the target.
        
- **Actions:**
    
    - **Multiattack:** Two mace attacks.
        
    - **Mace:** +4 to hit, 1d6+2 bludgeoning.
### 🐺 Wolf Chimera (Street Hunter)

_Base: **Polar Bear** (CR 2)_ _A horrifying fusion of a human and a polar wolf. It stands on its hind legs, towering over civilians, with quills poking through its white fur._

- **AC:** 12 (Natural Armor)
    
- **HP:** 42 (5d10 + 15)
    
- **Speed:** 40 ft., Swim 30 ft.
    
- **STR:** 20 (+5) | **DEX:** 10 (+0) | **CON:** 16 (+3) | **INT:** 2 (-4) | **WIS:** 13 (+1) | **CHA:** 7 (-2)
    
- **Skills:** Perception +3
    
- **Senses:** Passive Perception 13
    
- **Traits:**
    
    - **Keen Smell:** Advantage on Perception checks relying on smell.
        
- **Actions:**
    
    - **Multiattack:** The chimera makes two attacks: one with its Bite and one with its Claws.
        
    - **Bite:** +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 9 (1d8 + 5) piercing damage.
        
    - **Claws:** +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 12 (2d6 + 5) slashing damage.

### 🐗 Boar Chimera (Siege Breaker)

_Base: **Giant Boar** (CR 2)_ _A massive, bloated monstrosity. Its human face is stretched across a pig’s snout, and its tusks are jagged metal scraps fused into bone._

- **AC:** 12 (Natural Armor)
    
- **HP:** 42 (5d10 + 15)
    
- **Speed:** 40 ft.
    
- **STR:** 17 (+3) | **DEX:** 10 (+0) | **CON:** 16 (+3) | **INT:** 2 (-4) | **WIS:** 7 (-2) | **CHA:** 5 (-3)
    
- **Senses:** Passive Perception 8
    
- **Traits:**
    
    - **Charge:** If the chimera moves at least 20 ft. straight toward a target and hits with a tusk attack on the same turn, the target takes an extra 7 (2d6) slashing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a **DC 13 Strength saving throw** or be knocked **prone**.
        
    - **Relentless (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest):** If the chimera takes 7 damage or less that would reduce it to 0 hit points, it is reduced to 1 hit point instead.
        
- **Actions:**
    
    - **Tusk:** +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 10 (2d6 + 3) slashing damage.

### 🦅 Crow Chimera (Aerial Ambusher)

_Base: **Griffon** (CR 2)_ _A flock of these circles the mist. They have the bodies of giant crows but the grasping arms of men hanging from their chests._

- **AC:** 12
    
- **HP:** 59 (7d10 + 21)
    
- **Speed:** 30 ft., Fly 80 ft.
    
- **STR:** 18 (+4) | **DEX:** 15 (+2) | **CON:** 16 (+3) | **INT:** 2 (-4) | **WIS:** 13 (+1) | **CHA:** 8 (-1)
    
- **Skills:** Perception +5
    
- **Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 15
    
- **Traits:**
    
    - **Keen Sight:** Advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight.
        
- **Actions:**
    
    - **Multiattack:** The chimera makes two attacks: one with its Beak and one with its Claws.
        
    - **Beak:** +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 8 (1d8 + 4) piercing damage.
        
    - **Claws:** +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 11 (2d6 + 4) slashing damage.

### 🦗 Mantis Chimera (Mist Guardian)

_Base: **Hook Horror** (CR 3)_ _Encased in green chitin, this creature guards the Eggbank. It walks on two legs, and its arms end in massive, scythe-like hooks._

- **AC:** 15 (Natural Armor)
    
- **HP:** 75 (10d10 + 20)
    
- **Speed:** 30 ft., Climb 30 ft.
    
- **STR:** 18 (+4) | **DEX:** 10 (+0) | **CON:** 15 (+2) | **INT:** 6 (-2) | **WIS:** 12 (+1) | **CHA:** 7 (-2)
    
- **Skills:** Perception +3
    
- **Senses:** Blindsight 60 ft. (Echolocation), Darkvision 10 ft., Passive Perception 13
    
- **Traits:**
    
    - **Echolocation:** The chimera can't use its blindsight while deafened.
        
- **Actions:**
    
    - **Multiattack:** The chimera makes two Hook attacks.
        
    - **Hook:** +6 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 11 (2d6 + 4) piercing damage.
