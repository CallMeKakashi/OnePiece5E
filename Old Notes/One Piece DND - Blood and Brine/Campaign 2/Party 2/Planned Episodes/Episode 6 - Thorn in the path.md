# 🏴‍☠️ Episode 6: Thorn in the Road

**Session Goal:** Navigate the falling city, face the tragic consequences of the Mist, and make the final choice: Save the island, save the crewmate, or save yourselves.

---

### 🎬 Scene 1: South City – The Swordsman & The Traitor

**Location:** The main thoroughfare of South City. The mist is ankle-deep here but rising. Panic is absolute. **The Event:** The party sees **Goru** (the swordsman ally) surrounded by 4 **Wolf Chimeras**. He is exhausted, bleeding, and trying to protect a group of cowering civilians who (by luck of the 30%) haven't turned yet.

**Choice A: Ignore & Run**

- They sneak past. They hear Goru scream as he is overwhelmed. The path to the Old City is open, but they lose a potential ally for good.
    

**Choice B: Join the Fight**

- **Combat:** The party engages the Wolf Chimeras (Use _Polar Bear_ stats).
    
- **The Turn:** Mid-fight, a massive shockwave blasts two Chimeras away. **Nyx** (Goru’s brother/Former Enemy) lands.
    
- **Roleplay:** Nyx isn't helping out of kindness. He's mocking Goru.
    
    - _Nyx:_ "Still swinging that dull blade to save trash, brother? You're pathetic. There's a way to save the people _and_ the beasts, but you're too weak to see it."
        
- **The Hand-Off:** Nyx cuts a path for the civilians. Goru, clutching his side, looks at the party:
    
    - _Goru:_ "I'll hold this line with... him. Go to the **Eggbank**! There's a Marine Dispensary there. If there's a cure, it's in their vault. GO!"
        

---

### 🎬 Scene 2: Old City – Friends in Low Places

**Location:** The familiar slum streets of Old City. **The Encounter:** The party runs into **Rob** and **Flint**, the two bandits they befriended earlier. **The Horror:** They have mutated.

- **Rob (The Brawler):** Upper body has swelled into a massive **Beetle-like carapace**. One arm is a crushing pincer.
    
- **Flint (The Gunner):** His skin is covered in **Porcupine quills**, and his eyes are reptilian slits.
    

**The Interaction:** They aren't mindless beasts yet. They are in agony.

- _Rob (choking):_ "Boss... it hurts... my skin... it's too tight..."
    
- _Flint (twitching):_ "Run... we... can't stop... hunger..."
    

**Combat/Resolution:**

- **Rob-Chimera (Use _Hook Horror_ stats):** High AC, tries to grapple/crush.
    
- **Flint-Chimera (Use _Manticore_ stats without flight):** Fires tail spikes (quills) from a distance.
    
- **The Tragic Choice:** The party must knock them unconscious (very hard), kill them (mercy), or tie them up while dodging attacks.
    
    - _If killed:_ Rob's last words are thanking them.
        
    - _If spared:_ They writhe in the mud, fully succumbing to the beast mind.
        

---

### 🎬 Scene 3: The Eggbank Hub – The Crossroads

**Location:** The central plaza. To the North: **Marine Dispensary**. To the East: **Fish Market/Silk Court**. To the West: **Docks**. **The Situation:** The mist is thickest here. The party has a moment to breathe and decide their fate.

#### ⚔️ Path A: The Cure (Marine Dispensary)

- **The Objective:** Raid the dispensary for the antidote Goru mentioned.
    
- **The Obstacle:** A squad of **Marines** (Captain + 6 Guards).
    
    - _Why they are safe:_ They never drank the local water. They are guarding the cure for _themselves_ and the Nobles, under orders to let the "purge" happen.
        
- **Combat:** Tactical breach. The Marines have muskets and formation tactics.
    
- **Reward:** **The Antidote Case.** Enough to disperse into the air filtration system (at the Silk Court) or cure individuals (like Rob/Flint/Dravos).
    

#### 🐟 Path B: The Missing Piece (Fish Market)

- **The Objective:** Find Fenris.
    
- **The Scene:** The Market is silent. No mist yet (different water source), but signs of a brawl.
    
- **The Intel:** A terrified fishmonger hides in a barrel. He tells Shako:
    
    - "The Marine Lieutenant... **Leon**. He saw the sniper (Fenris) looking for his monkey. Leon laughed, said he needed a 'pet' too. He knocked the boy out, put him in seastone cuffs, and took him to a government ship... they left an hour ago."
        
- **Result:** Fenris is **gone**. The party realizes they have to leave the island to chase him eventually, but do they save the island first?
    

#### 🏰 Path C: The Class War (Silk Court Gates)

- **The Objective:** Help the Fish Market refugees enter the safe zone.
    
- **The Scene:** A massive steel gate blocks the Silk Court. High walls.
    
- **The Conflict:** 50+ refugees are pounding on the gates. **Royal Guards** stand on the ramparts, muskets aimed down.
    
- **The Action:** The party can blow the gate (Explosives/Strength), scale the wall and open it, or intimidate the guards.
    
    - _Consequence:_ If opened, the refugees flood in, safe from the mist. If ignored, the mist rolls in from the Old City, and the screaming begins.
        

#### ⚓ Path D: The Escape (Western Docks)

- **The Objective:** Get to the _Rustwing_ and leave.
    
- **The Encounter:** Just before the docks, they find **Sana** (the little girl). She is sitting on a crate, coughing. Her arm is starting to turn gray and furred.
    
- **The Guilt:** She recognizes them.
    
    - _Sana:_ "Mr. Pirate? Did you find the medicine? It's getting hard to breathe..."
        
- **The Choice:**
    
    - **Leave:** They walk past her. She dies. They escape with Dravos.
        
    - **Stay:** They turn back to the Dispensary (Path A) to get the cure for her.

### 🎲 Dungeon Master Cheat Sheet

**Enemy Stat Reflavors (for VTT):**

- **Rob-Chimera:** Use **Hook Horror** (CR 3). _Describe as Beetle-Man._
    
- **Flint-Chimera:** Use **Manticore** (CR 3). _Describe as Porcupine-Man. Tail spikes = Quills._
    
- **Marines:** Use **Thug** (Melee) and **Scout** (Ranged).
    
- **Lt. Leon (Flashback/If needed):** Use **Knight** or **Veteran**.
    

**Key Item: The Antidote**

- **Effect:** If inserted into the Silk Court ventilation hub (requires Path A + Path C), it cures the mist over the whole city in 1 hour.
    
- **Single Use:** Can be used as an injection to instantly cure 1 person (Sana, Rob, or Dravos).
    

**Ending the Session:** The session likely ends _after_ they resolve the immediate path choice (e.g., they secure the cure, or they breach the gate, or they set sail).

- _Cliffhanger (If they leave):_ As the _Rustwing_ pulls away, they see the Mist swallow the Silk Court. Sana is gone.
    
- _Cliffhanger (If they stay):_ They hold the cure, but a massive shadow (The Source of the Mist) rises over the Eggbank.
