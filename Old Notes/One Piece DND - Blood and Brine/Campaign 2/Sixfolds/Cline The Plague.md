### **Devil Fruit: Sick-Sick Fruit (Eki Eki no Mi)**

_Paramecia-type_

---

### **Passive Traits**

**Toxic Aura (10 ft):**  
Enemies in range must succeed a DC 13 Con Save or have disadvantage on their next attack.

**Disease Reservoir:**  
Immune to all disease and poison. Can _sense illness_ within 60 ft.

---

### **Actions**

**1. Infectious Touch (Melee, 1/turn)**  
+4 to hit, 5 ft.  
**Hit:** 1d6 poison damage + DC 13 Con Save or target is **sickened** (disadvantage on attacks and ability checks) for 1 turn.

**2. Sick Fog (Recharge 5–6)**  
Creates a 15 ft. radius of green fog for 1 minute.  
Creatures entering or starting turn inside must make DC 13 Con Save or suffer one:

- 1: Disadvantage on attacks (nausea)
    
- 2: -10 ft movement (fatigue)
    
- 3: Hallucinate (can’t take reactions)
    

**3. Symptom Shot (Ranged, 60 ft)**  
+4 to hit  
**Hit:** 1d8 + 2 poison damage  
On natural 18–20, target also becomes poisoned (1 turn).

---

### **Reactions**

**Contagion Backlash (1/short rest)**  
When hit by a melee attack, can release a pulse of sickness. Attacker must make DC 13 Con Save or be poisoned until end of their next turn.

---

### **Bonus Actions**

**Suppress Illness (self or ally within 5 ft)**  
Can suppress 1 condition (poisoned, exhaustion level, or nausea) for 1 minute.  
Does not remove it, just suppresses.
