### **Fusion Fusion Fruit (Fyu-Fyu no Mi) - Paramecia-type**

- **Ability:** Allows Ronan to fuse with another living person (only with _mutual consent_), or _freely fuse_ with inanimate objects without consent.
    
- **Living Fusion Conditions:**
    
    - Both Ronan and the target must clearly say "**Link Start!**" **at the same time**.
        
    - A **D20 roll** determines how powerful or stable the fusion is:
        
        - **1–5:** Unstable fusion (weak, clumsy, maybe side effects like headaches, loss of control).
            
        - **6–14:** Decent fusion (stronger than either individual, but fusion may break if heavily damaged).
            
        - **15–19:** Strong fusion (very powerful, enhanced traits, almost no drawbacks).
            
        - **Natural 20:** **Perfect Fusion** (legendary fusion, combines abilities _flawlessly_ and grants extra bonuses like fusion-exclusive moves or transformations).
            
- **Inanimate Object Fusion:**
    
    - No roll needed — Ronan can **instantly fuse** with objects (like weapons, armor, even things like ships or boulders) to gain abilities from them.
        
    - Examples:
        
        - Fusing with a sword to become a sword-armed fighter.
            
        - Fusing with a cannon to launch himself as a human cannonball.
            

### ❗ **Limitations:**

- Only one active fusion with a living being at a time.
    
- Fusion with living beings automatically ends if either participant falls unconscious.
    
- Fusion with objects can last indefinitely, but prolonged fusion may physically exhaust Ronan.
