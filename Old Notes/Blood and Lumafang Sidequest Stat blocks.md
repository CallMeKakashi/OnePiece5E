## ⚓ Captain Reddan Korr, Corrupt Marine Officer

_Medium Humanoid (Human), Lawful Evil, CR 8_

**AC** 17 (Half Plate, Lumafang Gauntlets) **HP** 152 (16d8 + 80) **Speed** 30 ft.  
STR 18 (+4) DEX 14 (+2) CON 20 (+5) INT 12 WIS 14 CHA 15 (+2)  
**Saves** Str +7, Con +8, Wis +5 **Skills** Athletics +7, Intimidation +5  
**Resistances** Fire, Psychic (when serum active)

### Traits

**Serum Gauntlets.** Once per combat, as bonus action inject Lumafang — for 1 minute gains:

- +2 AC, +2 to damage rolls, advantage on Str checks,
    
- loses 5 HP each round (start of turn).
    

**Tactical Mind.** Can use Help as a bonus action.

### Actions

**Multiattack.** 3 attacks with Saber or Gauntlet.

- **Saber.** +7 to hit, 5 ft., 11 (1d10 + 4) slashing + 1d6 fire.
    
- **Gauntlet Strike.** +7 to hit, 5 ft., 10 (1d8 + 4) bludgeoning + 1d6 fire + DC 15 Con save or stunned until end of next turn.  
    **Command Volley (Recharge 5-6).** Allies within 30 ft. make one attack as reaction.
    

### Bonus Actions

**Serum Injection.** (see above)  
**Marine Roar (1/day).** Each enemy that can hear must DC 15 Wis save or frightened 1 round.

> **Tactics:** Starts calm, then injects when below half HP; fight ends on burning deck.
