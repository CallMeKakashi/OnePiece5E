# **Wind-Taker Scout (CR 3)**

_Agile glider thief working in pairs._

**Medium Humanoid (Human), Chaotic Neutral**  
**AC** 14 (leather + glider harness)  
**HP** 45 (6d8+18)  
**Speed** 30 ft., **Glide** 60 ft. (see traits)

**STR** 10 (+0) | **DEX** 18 (+4) | **CON** 16 (+3) | **INT** 10 (+0) | **WIS** 12 (+1) | **CHA** 10 (+0)

### **Saving Throws** Dex +6, Wis +3

### **Skills** Acrobatics +6, Perception +3, Stealth +6, Survival +3

### **Senses** passive Perception 13

### **Languages** Common

### **CR** 3 (700 XP)

---

### **Traits**

**Wind Glider.**  
As long as the scout begins its turn in the air or elevated, it can glide 60 ft. horizontally and descend no more than 10 ft. It cannot fall unless its speed is reduced to 0.

**Gust Sense.**  
The scout has advantage on Dexterity saving throws against effects that move or push.

**High-Wind Footwork.**  
Opportunity attacks against the scout have disadvantage.

---

### **Actions**

**Multiattack.** The scout makes two attacks with its Hooked Claws or its Compressed Air Knife.

**Hooked Claws.**  
_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
_Hit:_ 8 (1d8+4) slashing.

**Compressed Air Knife (Ranged).**  
_Ranged Weapon Attack:_ +6 to hit, range 20/60 ft.  
_Hit:_ 9 (1d10+4) force.

**Wind Step (Recharge 5–6).**  
The scout dashes up to 40 ft. without provoking opportunity attacks and gains advantage on its next attack.

**AC** 16 (glider harness + hardened padding)

### **Skills** Acrobatics +9, Perception +6, Intimidation +5

### **CR** 6 (2,300 XP)

---

### **Traits**

**Master of the Winds.**  
Roci has advantage on all Dex saving throws, and ranged attacks against him have disadvantage while he is gliding.

When Roci is hit by an attack, he can reduce the damage by 1d10+6 and push himself 10 ft.

**Wind Glider Expert.**

**Echo Cave Awareness.**

---

+9 to hit, reach 5 ft.

+9 to hit.

# **4. Environmental Hazard — The Whistling Cave (No CR, but use as encounter)**

### **Sound Pressure Feedback**

Any loud noise (explosions, thunder, shouts, gunshots) triggers a **resonance shockwave**.

**Trigger:** A character performs an action louder than normal talking.

Creatures within 15 ft. must make a DC 14 Con save.

- Fail: 10 (3d6) thunder damage & deafened for 1 round
    
- Success: half, no deafness
    

Roci and Wind-Takers have advantage on this save.

---

### **Wind Direction Shifts**

Every round roll a **d4**:

1. Strong push left — DC 14 Str save or pushed 10 ft.
    
2. Strong push right — same
    
3. Updraft — jumping/leaping checks have advantage
    
4. Downdraft — gliding speed halved
    

Perfect for chaotic movement fights.
