## Description

Campaign-registered **Logia** devil fruit (**Slime-Slime**). Current eater: [[Droven Calligos]] — Blackhand 2nd Fleet right hand ("Remnant"). The body becomes living slime: fluid, adhesive, and chemically versatile.

## Powers

- **Slime-Slime: Amorphous Body** — Transform the whole body into slime—the Logia elemental form.
- **Slime-Slime: Living Vat** — Engulf people inside his slime mass; hold, constrict, or carry them within the body.
- **Slime-Slime: Keyhole Flow** — Ooze, squeeze, and thread the body through gaps far too narrow for a solid form.
- **Slime-Slime: Pseudopod Lash** — Extend limbs or slime tendrils for ranged melee strikes.
- **Slime-Slime: Catalyst Lob** — Throw slime projectiles charged with acid, poison, or other fluids—limited only by compositions [[Droven Calligos]] understands and can prepare.
- **Slime-Slime: Mending Gel** — Heal wounds by applying restorative slime to himself or others.
- **Slime-Slime: Smallest Speck** — After blunt force scatters him, any surviving blob can draw the mass back together and fully reconstruct the body.
- **Slime-Slime: Cushion State** — While in slime form, blunt impacts cannot harm him; blows disperse or pass through the mass without injury (reconstruction covers what scattering would otherwise cost).

## Drawbacks

- **Ocean's Scorn** — the eater cannot swim; running water and seastone apply as in [[What Are Devil Fruits]].
- **Haki** — armament haki can bypass logia intangibility and slime defenses where noted in session.
- **Elemental body** — logia intangibility unless struck with haki or countered by the element.
