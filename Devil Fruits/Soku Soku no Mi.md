## Description

Campaign-registered **Paramecia** devil fruit (**Speed-Speed**). Current eater: [[Veyl Corven]]. His motif is the modern-jazz theme *Atsumu Miya*; active techniques are channeled as **Jazz Style**—named after contemporary jazz pieces and idioms rather than classical or metal genres.

## Powers

- **Speed-Speed** — Passive movement far beyond normal limits (table: walk 120 ft.).
- **Jazz Style: Skimming the Surface** — Run fast enough to sprint across open water as if it were solid ground.
- **Jazz Style: Freight Train** — Surge to a crushing velocity and barrel through obstacles, barriers, or foes in his path.
- **Jazz Style: Upper Air** — Sky-walk: run through open air by combining armament haki footing with Speed-Speed momentum (part haki, part fruit).
- **Jazz Style: Double Time** — The fruit compresses his tempo in combat—bonus actions, reactions, and turn structure effectively run at a higher action economy than normal.

## Upgraded abilities

After speed training (notably with [[Baptiste]] during downtime before the Lunarfold tournament), [[Veyl Corven]]:

- **Jazz Style: Caravan** — Carry more allies at once while moving at full Speed-Speed (campaign: ferrying [[Roma]] and [[Malphas]] in a pinch).
- **Jazz Style: Tempo Fuerte** — Baseline speed and reach increased across all Jazz Style techniques.
- **Jazz Style: Call and Response** — Once per rest, push the fruit to its limit and loan his current speed to one willing ally for a single turn.
- **Jazz Style: Shadow Play** — At full tempo, leave speed-blurred afterimages in his wake that confuse pursuit and targeting.
- **Jazz Style: Rim Shot** — When he snaps past the sound barrier, the passage detonates a concussive sonic boom along his line of movement.

## Drawbacks

- **Ocean's Scorn** — the eater cannot swim; running water and seastone apply as in [[What Are Devil Fruits]].
- **Haki** — armament haki can bypass many paramecia defenses where noted in session.
