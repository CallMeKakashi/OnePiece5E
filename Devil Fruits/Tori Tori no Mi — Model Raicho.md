## Description

Campaign-registered **mythical Zoan** devil fruit (**Raichō** — Japanese *thunderbird*). Current eater: [[Malphas]] ("The Waning Fist"), [[Lunarfolds]] navigator. The fruit was fed to him on a storm-locked plateau as he lay dying; lightning crawled across the rind and his feathers have sparked ever since. Foundry exports only generic **Hybrid Form** / **Full Beast Form** traits on [[Malphas]]—no named fruit techniques on the Live sheet yet; table specifics may live in Foundry activities. Lunarian flight and flame investiture are racial, not this fruit.

## Powers

- **Raichō: Hybrid Form** — Bonus-action transform (fruit usage). Mythical Zoan: temp HP equal to **four × level** on entry; attacks deal extra damage equal to proficiency bonus; thunderbird hybrid traits (flight, natural weapons, senses per the fruit's beast block).
- **Raichō: Full Beast Form** — Bonus-action full thunderbird. Mythical beast HP formula: **(7 × level) + Constitution modifier**; uses the fruit's Full Beast stat block while retaining mental stats and class features where the form allows.
- **Raichō: Static Plumage** — Feathers and wing-edges crackle with stored lightning; sparks jump to metal (campaign: seastone cage bars, bindings) and shock on contact.
- **Raichō: Thunderstroke** — Channel lightning through unarmed strikes and natural weapons—fists and talons land like thunder (campaign: "fists like thunder," electricity bleeding from every feather).
- **Raichō: Stormcall** — Bend weather overhead—local storm clouds, charged air, lightning arcing with unnatural precision (ties to the plateau where he ate the fruit).
- **Raichō: Skybreaker** — Fight from the air: dive attacks, hit-and-run jumps, and distance control against grounded foes.

## Upgraded abilities

Unlocked in play during the Soundless Five arc ([[Sessions/Session 17 - Howling Thunder|Session 17 — Howling Thunder]] prep / table):

- **Raichō: Enhanced Form (Second Form)** — When a storm he has called meets conductive material (e.g. dust from his kit), lightning erupts and he snaps into a deeper thunderbird form—**Enhanced Form** benefits (sourcebook: chosen hybrid or beast form, +5 ft. reach, +1d8 damage, optional size increase; at table: refilled to max HP plus extra temporary HP). Once per long rest unless leveled to standard Enhanced Form cadence.

## Drawbacks

- **Ocean's Scorn** — the eater cannot swim; running water and seastone apply as in [[What Are Devil Fruits]].
- **Haki** — armament haki can bypass zoan durability and natural defenses where noted in session.
- **Seastone** — cuffs or dust-laced bars shut down transformation and lightning until contact breaks (auction capture).
