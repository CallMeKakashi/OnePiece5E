## Description

Campaign-registered **Paramecia** devil fruit. Current eater: [[Baptiste]].

## Powers

- **Uchu Uchu: Void Step** — brief teleportation (Misty Step reskin): blink to an unoccupied space he can see.
- **Uchu Uchu: Night Sky** — space-void darkness field (Darkness reskin) to deny sight and muffle the area.
- **Uchu Uchu: Void Field** — force barrier (Shield reskin) to absorb harm until it fades.
- **Uchu Uchu: Gravity Control (Haste)** — alter local time/weight to accelerate a target and grant it extra action pressure.
- **Uchu Uchu: Gravity Control (Slow)** — drag time/effort down to hinder multiple enemies (speed reduction + reaction denial).
- **Uchu Uchu: Air Vaccumm (Suffocate)** — suffocating vacuum pull: restrains a target and chokes its ability to breathe.
- **Uchu Uchu: Black Hole (Gravity Sinkhole)** — crushing gravity sphere that tugs creatures toward a center.
- **Uchu Uchu: Steel Wind Strike (Void Step Improved)** — blink-strike sequence: attack multiple targets, then teleport to one.
- **Uchu Uchu: To the Void (Banishment)** — banish a creature into a harmless demiplane, preventing it from acting.
- **Uchu Uchu: Dark Star (Night Sky Improved)** — expanded darkness + crushing gravitational zone that denies vision and sound.
- **Uchu Uchu: Reality Break (To the Void Improved)** — reality-warp debuff that blocks reactions (and destabilizes the target’s turn-to-turn options).
- **Uchu Uchu: Ravenous Void (Black Hole Improved)** — larger sustained gravity void that destroys or drags nonmagical objects.

## Drawbacks

- **Ocean's Scorn** — the eater cannot swim; running water and seastone apply as in [[What Are Devil Fruits]].
- **Haki** — armament haki can bypass many paramecia defenses where noted in session.
