## Description

Campaign-registered **Logia** devil fruit (**Ink-Ink**). Current eater: [[Rhum Guiseppi]].

## Powers

- **Ink spot** — Vanish into an ink spot as small as a dot.
- **Free flow** — Move the spot where he chooses and step in or out of it.

## Drawbacks

- **Ocean's Scorn** — the eater cannot swim; running water and seastone apply as in [[What Are Devil Fruits]].
- **Haki** — armament haki can bypass many paramecia defenses where noted in session.
