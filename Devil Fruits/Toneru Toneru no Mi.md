## Description

Campaign-registered **special Paramecia** devil fruit (**Tunnel-Tunnel**)—unusually potent from the first bite, with a ceiling that scales sharply with the eater's own skill and imagination. Current eater: [[Dravos]] ("The Mole"), navigator for the [[Gentle Giant Pirates]]; a conjuration-trained wizard who expresses the fruit through **PVC pipe** tunnels, portals, and utility work (see his Live sheet). What he can pull from the fruit at table is only partly listed here; non-fruit class features stay on [[Dravos]].

## Powers

- **Tunnel-Tunnel: Get Me Out** — Briefly surrounded by pipe-work, teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space he can see.
- **Tunnel-Tunnel: Thousand-Mile Tunnel** — Long-range transit: teleport himself and up to eight willing creatures he can see, or one Large-or-smaller object, to a destination he knows on the same plane (success scales with familiarity).
- **Tunnel-Tunnel: Linked Portals** — Open two Large ground portals within range—mist-filled rings; one-way passage between them for the duration.
- **Tunnel-Tunnel: Redirect** — When a seen attacker strikes him, swap places with a willing creature within range; the ally becomes the target instead.
- **Tunnel-Tunnel: Wall of Pipes** — Raise an invisible wall of force as flat panels, a dome, or a sphere (pipe-flavored conjuration).
- **Tunnel-Tunnel: Pipe Vines** — Conjure a vine from a surface; melee strike for bludgeoning damage and pull a target toward it (Grappled on hit, Huge or smaller).
- **Tunnel-Tunnel: Pipe Network** — Ranged pipe-web restrains a Large-or-smaller creature until it breaks free or the web is destroyed (vulnerable to fire, immune to bludgeoning).
- **Tunnel-Tunnel: Catch Me If You Can** — Touch a willing creature; it becomes mist with 10 ft. fly, can occupy another creature's space, and gains resistance to nonmagical damage plus advantage on Str/Dex/Con saves until the effect ends or it drops to 0 HP.
- **Tunnel-Tunnel: Keep Me High** — Levitate one seen creature or object (up to 500 lb.) up to 20 feet; it can climb along surfaces while suspended.
- **Tunnel-Tunnel: Run Away** — On casting and as a bonus action each turn until the effect ends, take the Dash action.
- **Tunnel-Tunnel: Catch Me** — Slow the fall of up to five falling creatures; no fall damage and they land on their feet if they land before the effect ends.
- **Tunnel-Tunnel: Pipes Protect Me** — Touch a willing unarmored creature; its base AC becomes 13 + Dexterity modifier until armor is donned or the effect is dismissed.
- **Tunnel-Tunnel: Blast** — 15-foot cube of thunder force centered on him; Constitution save or thunder damage and push; unsecured objects in the area shoved outward.
- **Tunnel-Tunnel: Wind Pipe** — Bend air at a seen point—push a Medium-or-smaller creature (save), shove a light object, or create a harmless sensory effect.
- **Tunnel-Tunnel: Tunnel** — Shape or excavate dirt/stone in a 5-foot cube (instant dig/move earth, or brief patterns/inscriptions on stone).
- **Tunnel-Tunnel: Water Hose** — Manipulate water in a 5-foot cube (flow, simple animated shapes, color/opacity).
- **Tunnel-Tunnel: Pipe Wrench** — Conjure a massive two-handed pipe wrench for melee use.

## Drawbacks

- **Ocean's Scorn** — the eater cannot swim; running water and seastone apply as in [[What Are Devil Fruits]].
- **Haki** — armament haki can bypass many paramecia defenses where noted in session.
- **Eater-bound** — range, precision, and which techniques are available in play depend on the user's training and cleverness; the fruit supplies the tunnel vocabulary, not mastery for free.
