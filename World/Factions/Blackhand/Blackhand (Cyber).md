## ⚔️ **Blackhand Cane – Weaponized Form**

**Alias:** _"The Ghost of Virellis"_  
**Type:** Cyborg (Germa-enhanced)  
**Former Role:** Mentor to Astor  
**Current Role:** Enforcer of Lord Halric & Commodore Briggs

---

### 📜 Overview

Once a feared but honorable swordsman, **Blackhand Cane** was thought dead. Instead, he was captured by Jerma and turned into a mechanized servant—stripped of his free will and repurposed as a living weapon. His signature sword _Chiha_ remains with him, fused into his new body.

Only flickers of his old self remain—buried under programming, trauma, and suppressants.

---

### 🧠 Personality (Now)

- **Emotionless** on the surface: follows orders with cold precision.
    
- **Flickers of memory** occur when he sees Astor, the Chiha sword, or anything from his past.
    
- Occasionally **hesitates** mid-attack—but overrides it.
    
- **Silent** most of the time. When he speaks, it’s glitchy and robotic.
    

---

### 🦾 Design

- **Appearance:**
    
    - One arm entirely mechanical (hence “Blackhand”), reinforced with Haki-conductive alloy.
        
    - His jaw is replaced with a vocalizer. Eyes glow faint red.
        
    - Torso contains **Jerma’s neural override tech**—visible like a cybernetic spine.
        
    - Chiha is fused into his right arm; it emerges or retracts on command.
        
    - Wears remnants of his old coat, scorched and sewn with Germa markings.
        

---

### 🛠️ Stat Block Summary (CR 6 – Mini Boss)

**AC:** 17 (Reinforced body)  
**HP:** ~95 (11d8+44)  
**Speed:** 40 ft  
**Proficiency Bonus:** +3

#### **STR 18** | DEX 14 | CON 18 | INT 9 | WIS 11 | CHA 6

---

### 🗡️ Features & Abilities

#### **Chiha Core Blade** _(Legendary Sword)_

- Counts as magical and Haki-compatible.
    
- **+7 to hit**, **1d10+4 slashing**, plus **1d6 force damage**
    
- On crit, applies **“Core Wound”**: target’s max HP reduced by 5 until healed magically.
    

---

#### **Overdrive Mode** _(Recharge 5–6)_

- For 2 turns, gains +2 AC, advantage on attacks, and movement speed doubled.
    
- Drawback: Takes 10 damage after Overdrive ends.
    

---

#### **Suppressing Protocol (1/day)**

- A wave of fear-inducing pulses. All enemies within 15 ft must make **DC 15 Wis Save** or be **frightened** for 1 round.
    
- Astor has **disadvantage** on this save due to personal connection.
    

---

#### **Reluctant Echo (Passive)**

When Astor is nearby, there’s a **5% chance per round** that Blackhand hesitates and skips an attack.

---

#### **Phase-Lock Grapple**

- Can shoot mechanical wires from arm to **pull a target** (30 ft range)
    
- On failed Dex save (DC 14), target is pulled to melee range and **restrained** until escape (DC 14 Str).
    

---

### 🧠 Weaknesses

- **Emotion Flashbacks**: If Astor uses his full name, tells a memory, or shows Chiha’s original scabbard, he must roll **DC 15 Wisdom Save** or be stunned for 1 round. (Once per encounter)
    
- **Germa Node**: Hidden in his spine. A precise hit (DC 18 called shot) disables Overdrive and Frightening Protocol for the rest of combat.
    

---

### 🧨 Legendary Actions (2 per round)

- **Blade Whirl (1 action):** Attacks up to two targets within 5 ft.
    
- **Suppressive Slam (1 action):** Deals 2d8 bludgeoning + knocks prone if target fails DC 13 Dex save.
    
- **Engage Target (1 action):** Instantly moves 30 ft toward one visible target, ignoring attacks of opportunity.
