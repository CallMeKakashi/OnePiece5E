## ⚓ Background — _“The Mutiny of the Black Comet”_

> “Some songs are meant to be sung once — before the sea swallows them whole.”

### 🏴‍☠️ Early Life

Red (then Quill) left Zou young, chasing stories of grand adventure.  
He joined the **Black Comet Pirates**, led by **Captain Tusk**, a boar-faced mink infamous in East Blue.  
Red’s musical gift and free spirit made him a favorite among the crew — until Tusk’s greed consumed everything.

---

### 💥 The Mutiny

A Devil Fruit discovery aboard the **Black Comet** sparked rebellion.  
Tusk hoarded it for himself, executing dissenters.  
Quill and **Jack Sparrow** (a bird-faced pirate) led a mutiny to stop him.

**Outcome:**

- Crew split nearly 50/50.
    
- Jack rallied the rebels; Quill hesitated, trying to spare lives.
    
- The mutiny failed.
    
- Jack was captured.
    
- Quill was beaten nearly to death — and lost his memories.

## 🧠 Memory Flashbacks (Campaign Triggers)

These moments return as Red’s memories slowly resurface in “Blood and Brine.”  
DM prompts are marked 1–4; Red recalls these as partial visions or dreams.

---

### **Flashback 1 – “Storm Before the Silence”**

_Setting:_ Deck of the Black Comet, calm sea before mutiny.

> **Jack:** “You ever think about jumpin’ ship?”  
> **Red:** “Every week. But something always pulls me back.”  
> **Jack:** “Like Tusk?”  
> **Red:** “Like you, idiot.”

A rare calm night — foreshadowing the storm to come.

---

### **Flashback 2 – “The Whispered Pact”**

_Setting:_ Below deck, candlelight and whispered plans.  
Jack and a small circle plot Tusk’s downfall.

> **Mink Crewman:** “You sure Red’s with us?”  
> **Jack:** “He doesn’t have to be. But I won’t leave him behind.”  
> **Red (entering):** “You bastards really gonna try this without me? Count me in.”

---

### **Flashback 3 – “The Mutiny Burns”**

_Setting:_ Deck chaos. Blood, thunder, cannonfire.

> **Red (to Tusk):** “Just shut down the mutiny and let them go. It’s me you want.”  
> **Tusk:** _[roaring]_ “You don’t speak for them.”

Red takes a blow to the head — the world fades.

---

### **Flashback 4 – “The Chain and the Choice”**

_Setting:_ Ship’s brig. Jack chained, Red half-conscious.

> **Tusk:** “He thought you were his brother. And you sold him out.”  
> **Jack:** “I didn’t sell him. I gambled. And we both lost.”

Red blacks out — memory fractured ever since.

---

## 🪶 Post-Mutiny — “The Songbird Drifts”

- Red awakens on a derelict boat, adrift and half-dead.
    
- He no longer remembers **Tusk**, **Jack**, or the **Black Comet**.
    
- Drifts between ports as a **street musician**, earning coin through song and charm.
    
- Eventually meets **Blackhand Cane’s Crew**, who take him in for his talent and humor.
    
- Now known simply as **“Red.”**
    

---

## 🍻 Personality & Quirks

- **Heart:** Playful, charismatic, loyal — hides his pain behind jokes.
    
- **Quirk:** Sleeps in ship masts; claims “the air tastes different up there.”
    
- **Speech:** Casual, teasing, sings mid-sentence sometimes.
    
- **Phobia:** Deep unease around chains or cages (residual trauma).
    
- **Habit:** Hums to fill silence; can’t stand quiet — too many ghosts in it.
