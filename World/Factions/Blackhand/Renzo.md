## Description

An old but burly hammerhead shark fishman. Despite his age, he carries himself with the quiet authority of someone who has seen every kind of fight the sea has to offer.

## Role

Right-hand man of [[Blackhand Cane]]. Serves as a senior operative in the [[Blackhand]] crew, handling recruitment, logistics, and front-line combat. Trained [[Ronan]] in Fishman Karate. Provided the ship referenced in [[🦾 Saplea D. Isla — "Dreadnaught"|Saplea D. Isla]]'s logbook entry.

## Personal Quests

- Serving Blackhand Cane and holding the crew together
- Scouting and recruiting talent for the Blackhand fleets

## Backstory

Renzo has been at Blackhand Cane's side for years, one of the veteran pillars of the organization. He was present during the raid on the Germa facility where [[Baptiste]] was rescued, fighting alongside [[Morley]], [[Tray]], and others.

He recruited [[Shako]] as shipwright after watching him fight in the underground pits of Yobatsuru Port. Renzo saw past the blood-sport spectacle to the craftsman's hands beneath — and paid off Shako's fight promoter **Boro** to free him from his contract.

Renzo also trained [[Ronan]] in Fishman Karate during an earlier period of the crew's history.
