# Mira the Unbreakable

### **Backstory**

Mira was rescued at the age of **10** by Blackhand from a slave ship en route to Mariejois. Even as a child, her spirit was unyielding — she was a fighter through and through.

She sailed alongside Blackhand for **20 years**, learning the true essence of **Haki** directly from him and mastering **Fishman Karate** under Raizo, the 3rd Fleet Commander. Over time, her unwavering spirit and raw power earned her the epithet **“Mira the Unbreakable.”**

It is said she once withstood **two weeks of torture at the hands of CP0**, yet never revealed a single word that would endanger Blackhand or her crew. She eventually broke out on her own, aided by her closest allies — the “Fleet”: **Tray, Thompson, Celine, and Katherine**.

To them — and to many others — Mira is nothing short of a **god**, the one who showed them a better life, far away from the slums of an unnamed island deep within the New World.

---

### **Appearance**

- **Height:** 8 feet tall
    
- **Build:** Muscular but lean, exuding effortless power
    
- **Eyes:** Emerald green, sharp and commanding
    
- **Hair:** Wild, curly, deep red
    
- **Skin:** Deep tan
    
- **Clothing:** A striking robe in pink and green, flowing yet battle-ready
    
- **Weapons:** None — she doesn’t need them. Her hands alone are more than enough.
    

---

### **Abilities & Training**

- Mastery of **Fishman Karate** (learned from Raizo)
    
- All **Six Styles (Rokushiki)** techniques of CP agents (taught by Blackhand himself)
    
- Advanced Haki user — both Observation and Armament
    

---

### **Personality & Traits**

- Carries a **kind, almost motherly smile**, especially toward her family and crew — a warmth she perhaps learned from Blackhand himself. To them, she is an unshakable pillar and a nurturing protector.
    
- In stark contrast, she is utterly **ruthless toward her enemies**. Most marines tremble upon hearing that **"Mira the Unbreakable"** is coming for them.
    
- Commands immense respect and loyalty — a living embodiment of unwavering will and fierce love.
    

---

### **Legacy & Bounty**

Mira stands as the **4th Fleet Commander of Blackhand**, commanding immense respect both within and beyond the crew.

- **Current Bounty:** 125,000,000 berries
    
- **Reputation:** Feared by enemies, revered by her allies — her legend stretches across seas.
    

---

**Flavor quote:**

> "I will hold my family in my arms and crush my enemies under my fists. That's what it means to be unbreakable."
