Pirate crew connected to the [[Sessions/Sidequests/Spider's Nest]] sidequest.

## Members

- [[Simon The One Armed Tyrant]] — captain (formerly known as Silas), founding member of [[Motley Crew]]
- [[Ben]] — "Iron-Fist Breaker," bruiser with mechanical arm
- [[Chloe]] — "Eight-Legs Widow," wild card
- [[Daniel]] — "No-Knees Phantom," navigator

## Overview

The Spider Nest Pirates suffered heavy losses — most of the original officers are dead ("six feet under" per Daniel). After Silas fell and reemerged as [[Simon The One Armed Tyrant]], the remaining crew regrouped. Daniel, Ben, and Chloe briefly allied with the party to destroy [[Hallow]]'s G-45 facility and free Chloe.
