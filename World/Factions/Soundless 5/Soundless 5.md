## Overview

The Soundless Five are the named enforcers who uphold the silence laws of [[Decibella Kingdom]]. Under their rule, sound itself is criminal — citizens are punished for speaking, laughing, or making noise. Their power is challenged by the [[Decibella Revolutionary]] resistance movement. The party encountered them during the tower trials ([[Episode 14 - The Decibel Decree]]–[[Episode 16 - Broken Promises]]).
