Industrial/corporate faction connected to the G-45 facility, chimeras, and cogwork technology.

## Members

- [[Hallow]] — G-45 facility antagonist; Callisto station master (deceased)
- [[Dr Nikolai Tesla]] — lead scientist (Marine G-4.5 / Briggs patronage; Germa exile)
- [[RyeNera]] — two-human amalgam prototype (deceased)

## Overview

Mugen Industries operates industrial and research facilities — **G-45** (chimeras, cogwork core; [[Sessions/Sidequests/Spider's Nest]] / [[Episode 6 - Hallow's End]]) and **Callisto** (Nikolai's fusion program; later destroyed — [[Timeline/1478/1478-12moon [Newspaper] Grand Line Tribune — Morning]]).

Owned and run in the background by **Commodore Briggs** (G-4.5), who funds Nikolai's experiments including **[[RyeNera]]** and the stronger **[[Monster Manual/Aberrant Abomination|Aberrant Abomination]]** line toward a recreated "perfect being" from Virellis.
