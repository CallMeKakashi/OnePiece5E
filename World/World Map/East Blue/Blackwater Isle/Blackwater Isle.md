# Blackwater Isle

East Blue island west of [[Ambercrest]]. A fruit-farming island and **safe haven** under the protection of the [[Blackhand]] pirates.

## Overview

- **Vibe**: fertile orchards and farmsteads anchored by a quietly-busy port town; “safe water” by Blackhand reputation.
- **Tone**: pragmatic, coastal-island life with a protective pirate shadow—locals benefit from the trade and help when Blackhand returns.
- **Campaign use**: Blackhand-friendly staging ground before runs toward [[Ambercrest]] and [[Spirit Cliff]].

## Geography & climate

- **Coastline**: low coastal shelf with working docks and small beaches suitable for farm shipping and coastal runs.
- **Interior**: **orchards and rolling farmland** dominating the island’s usable land.
- **Weather**: frequent fog and sudden squalls (enough to make navigation treacherous).

## Settlements & ports

### Primary port

- **Name**: **Caneyard**
- **Docks**: built for quiet arrivals; lots of private piers, tucked slips, and “no questions” chandlers.
- **Services**: repairs (basic), illicit provisioning, rumor brokers, forged papers (if needed).

## Economy

- **Exports**: fruit and farm goods (orchard harvests; preserve goods and bulk shipments).
- **Imports**: ship parts, lamp oil, medicines, luxury liquor.
- **Black market**: stolen cargo laundering; contraband transshipment between islands.

## Hazards

- **Navigation**: fog banks + shifting shoals (easy to get turned around at night).
- **Wildlife**: the island used to suffer frequent **Sea King** attacks, but after Blackhand arrived **no Sea Kings have approached** (cause unknown; reputation, patrols, or something stranger).
- **Social**: betrayal culture; “hospitality” is transactional and reputation-driven.
