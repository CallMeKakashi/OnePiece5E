### ⏳ Hour 1: The Split Screen (High Intensity)

_DM Note: Cut back and forth between the two groups every 10–15 minutes to keep tension high._

#### Group A: The Chase (Malphas, Baptiste, Veyl)

**Objective:** Stop the **"Jester's Jet"** (Speedboat) and capture "Nitrous" Ned.

- **The Scene:** Malphas is clinging to the hull, Baptiste is flying above dodging "Confetti Flak," and Veyl is sprinting on the water using his Speed-Speed Fruit.
    
- **Mechanics (Complex Skill Challenge - 4 Successes before 3 Failures):**
    
    - **Veyl:** Needs to close the gap. _Athletics (Endurance)_ or _Acrobatics_ (DC 14) to handle the choppy wake.
        
    - **Malphas:** Needs to breach the cockpit. _Strength_ check to smash the reinforced glass or _Thieves' Tools_ to shimmy the hatch (DC 16).
        
    - **Baptiste:** Needs to disable the engine or driver without destroying the boat. _Ranged Attack_ (aiming for the engine exhaust) or _Intimidation_ (DC 15).
        
- **Outcome:**
    
    - _Success:_ Ned surrenders, crying. "Don't hurt the paint job!"
        
    - _Failure:_ Ned activates "Ejector Cargo," dropping explosive barrels. The party takes damage, but the boat stalls out, allowing capture.
        

#### Group B: The Sinking Circus (Serica, Bob, Roma)

**Objective:** Survive on the sinking tent and control the captives.

- **The Scene:** The tent is a flat canvas floating on the waves, slowly taking on water. The 3 captured clowns are tied up but rowdy.
    
- **The Captives:**
    
    1. **Bozo the Brute (Squad Leader):** Big, muscular, painted frown. Arrogant.
        
    2. **Giggles & Chuckles (Minions):** Twin acrobats, terrified of drowning.
        
- **The Problem:** The "Scent of Blood" (from the earlier fight) is attracting something from below.
    
- **Action:**
    
    - _Serica/Bob:_ Must make _Perception_ checks to notice bubbling in the water.
        
    - _Roleplay:_ The clowns start panicking. "Untie us! The Guardian is coming!"

### ⏳ Hour 2: The Convergence & The Sea Beast

**The Event:** Just as Group A (The Boat) returns to pick up Group B, the threat beneath the tent reveals itself.

#### Boss Encounter: The Giant Hermit Crab

- **The Twist:** The "odd-looking tent" wasn't just a tent—it was being eyed as a new shell by a **Giant Hermit Crab** (Size: Huge).
    
- **The Setup:** The Crab bursts from the water, lifting the tent (and the party) onto its back. It tries to drag the tent underwater.
    
- **Combat Dynamics:**
    
    - **Terrain:** The party is fighting _on top_ of the crab/tent while the boat circles around.
        
    - **The Boat:** Veyl/Malphas/Baptiste can fire ship cannons or board the crab to help.
        
    - **Hazard:** Every round (Initiative 20), the Crab submerges slightly. _DEX Save (DC 13)_ or slip into the water.
        
- **Victory Condition:** Deal enough damage to make it flee, or distract it with "Bait" (maybe the clowns?) to escape.

### ⏳ Hour 3: Interrogation & The "Smile" Connection

Once the crew is safe on the "Jester's Jet" (or towing a raft made of the tent), they turn their attention to the captives.

#### The Interrogation of "Nitrous" Ned & Bozo

**What they know (The Intel):**

1. **The Mission:** They were sent by **"Ringmaster Rictus"** to capture the "Animal People" of the reserve.
    
2. **The Market:** Since **Blackhand** disappeared, the "protection treaties" on this sea are null and void. "It's a free-for-all now! Slavers, organ dealers, Smile brokers—everyone is grabbing what they can."
    
3. **The Buyer:** They were supposed to deliver the slaves to a black market coordinate: **"The Gray Terminal"** (or a specific localized Black Market island).
    
4. **The Shadows Connection (CRITICAL):**
    
    - If pressed about _why_ they are so desperate, Bozo spills: "We aren't the only ones hunting! The **Shadows Guild** is mobilizing. We saw a **Shark Fishman** and a **Woman in Black** asking about a 'Demon' and a 'Angel' (Malphas/Baptiste) at the last port. We just wanted to get our payday and leave before _they_ arrived!"

### ⏳ Hour 4: Navigation & Downtime

**Objective:** Assess resources and plan the next move.

- **The Loot:**
    
    - **The Boat:** The _Jester's Jet_ is fast but burns fuel quickly. (Can hold the whole party, but it's a tight squeeze).
        
    - **The Log Pose:** Found on Ned. It points to the **Ringmaster's Flagship** (The "Big Top Barque").
        
    - **Beli:** 50,000 Beli in loose change.
        
    - **Strange Map:** A map of the local sector with big red "X" marks over islands that were previously under Blackhand's protection, now labeled "OPEN SEASON."
        
- **Party Decision:**
    
    - Do they chase the Ringmaster to stop the slave trade?
        
    - Do they flee to avoid The Shadows?
        
    - Do they try to contact their allies regarding Blackhand?

### ⏳ Hour 5: The Cliffhanger

**Scene:** The sun is setting. The party is exhausted, drifting on the open sea in their captured clown boat.

- **The Call:** The Transponder Snail on the boat rings. It's **Ringmaster Rictus**.
    
    - _Rictus:_ "Ned? You're late. The auction starts in 24 hours. If you lost my merchandise, I'll turn your skin into a tent."
        
- **The Sight:** As the call ends, the lookout (Serica or Veyl) spots a silhouette on the horizon.
    
    - It’s not a Marine ship. It’s a small, sleek sloop with a **Scorpion** painted on the sail.
        
    - _Cliffhanger:_ Through a spyglass, they see **Iron-Jaw Kael** standing on the bow, sniffing the air. He points directly at them. The hunt is on.
