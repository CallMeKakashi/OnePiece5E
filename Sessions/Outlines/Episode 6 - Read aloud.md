### 🎬 Scene 1: South City – The Breaking Point

**📜 Read Aloud (Arrival)**

> "As you step into the main thoroughfare of South City, the air changes. It’s no longer the fresh sea breeze of the Grand Line. It tastes of copper and rot. A sickly, bruising purple mist clings to the cobblestones, swirling around your ankles like a living thing.
> 
> The streets are a chaos of overturned carts and shattered glass. But the worst part is the sound. It isn’t the roar of a monster; it’s the wet, tearing sound of transformation, punctuated by the screams of those who haven't turned yet. Through the haze, you see a familiar silhouette—Goru, the swordsman. He stands alone against four massive, white-furred beasts, his blade trembling."

**🗣️ NPC Dialogue: Goru (The Exhausted Swordsman)**

- **To the Beasts:** "Back! I said get back! These people are under my protection!"
    
- **To the Party (Desperate):** "You... you made it? Don't come closer! The air—it's changing them! I can't hold them back much longer... there are too many!"
    

**🗣️ NPC Dialogue: Nyx (The Rival - Entrance)**

- _(After landing a massive shockwave attack)_: "Hah! Look at you, brother. Still swinging that dull piece of scrap metal to save trash? You're pathetic."
    
- **To the Party:** "Don't misunderstand, pirates. I'm not here for charity. But a city of beasts is boring. Go to the **Eggbank**. The Marines have a Dispensary there. If there's a cure, it's in their vault. Now move, before I change my mind and let the wolves eat you."

### 🎬 Scene 2: Old City – Friends in Low Places

**📜 Read Aloud (The Encounter)**

> "The Old City is darker. The buildings here lean over the street, blocking out the little sunlight that pierces the mist. It’s quiet here—too quiet. You see two shapes huddled near a smashed storefront. They are wearing familiar rags—red bandanas and patched leather vests.
> 
> It’s Rob and Flint. But... they look wrong. Rob’s back has split open, revealing a glistening, black beetle shell. One of his arms is swollen into a massive pincer. Flint is crouched on all fours, his skin erupting with long, needle-sharp quills. They turn to look at you, tears streaming down human faces that are slowly being swallowed by the monster within."

**🗣️ NPC Dialogue: Rob (The Beetle-Man)**

- _(Voice deep, choking, grappling with his own throat)_: "Boss...? Is that... you? It hurts... skin is... too tight..."
    
- _(Losing control)_: "Run... please... the hunger... it's loud... SO LOUD!"
    

**🗣️ NPC Dialogue: Flint (The Quill-Lizard)**

- _(Twitching, eyes darting wildly)_: "Did we... did we do good? We protected... the stash... right? Why... can't I... stand up?"
    
- _(Feral scream)_: "MEAT! NO! FRIENDS! MEAT!"

### 🎬 Scene 3: The Eggbank Hub (The Crossroads)

**📜 Read Aloud (The Hub)**

> "You reach the Eggbank Plaza. The mist here is thick, reducing visibility to a mere thirty feet. Shapes loom in the fog—massive shadows moving with unnatural gaits.
> 
> To your **North**, the heavy steel doors of the Marine Dispensary are barricaded, red lights flashing. To your **East**, the path splits toward the Fish Market and the high walls of the Silk Court. To your **West**, the wind carries the smell of salt—the Docks.
> 
> You stand at the center of the storm. Every second you waste, the purple fog rises higher."

Take one more step, and we open fire! For Justice, we cannot let the infection leave this island!"

#### 🐟 Path B: The Fish Market (Looking for Fenris)

**📜 Read Aloud (The Aftermath)**

> "The Fish Market is eerily untouched by the mist, protected by the natural wind tunnels of the coast. But it has been devastated by violence. Stalls are smashed to splinters. A massive crater sits in the center of the square. There is no sign of Fenris, only a lone Fishmonger hiding inside an empty tuna barrel."

**🗣️ NPC Dialogue: Terrified Fishmonger**

- _(Whispering)_: " The sniper? The one with the monkey? Aye... I saw him. He fought like a demon. But the Marine... **Lieutenant Leon**. He was too fast. He laughed... said he needed a 'pet' for his collection. He put the boy in seastone cuffs and dragged him to the sleek government ship. They left... maybe an hour ago? You're too late, pirates. He's gone."

#### 🏰 Path C: The Silk Court Gates

**📜 Read Aloud (The Class Divide)**

> "A crowd of fifty or more desperate civilians is pounding against the golden gates of the Silk Court. High above, on the white marble walls, Royal Guards look down, muskets aimed. The refugees are screaming, holding up children, begging to be let into the safe zone before the mist reaches them. The Guards do not flinch."

**🗣️ NPC Dialogue: Royal Guard Commander**

- _(Through a megaphone)_: "Disperse! The Silk Court is at capacity. Any attempt to breach the wall will be met with lethal force. Your sacrifice ensures the survival of the nobility. Go back to your homes and... accept your fate."

#### ⚓ Path D: The Escape (Western Docks)

**📜 Read Aloud (The Girl)**

> "The Western Docks are chaotic. People are fighting over rowboats. Ropes are being cut. As you sprint toward the _Rustwing_, you spot a small figure sitting on a crate near the gangplank.
> 
> It’s Sana, the little girl Fenris helped when you first arrived. She is clutching a ragged doll. Her left arm has turned gray and furry, and her breathing is a wet rattle. She looks up as you approach, her eyes glassy."

**🗣️ NPC Dialogue: Sana**

- _(Weak, smiling faintly)_: "Mr. Pirate? You came back? I knew you would... Fenris said you were heroes."
    
- _(Coughs, purple smoke escaping her lips)_: "Did you find the medicine? My chest feels heavy... like a big rock is sitting on it. I don't want to turn into a monster... it scares me."
