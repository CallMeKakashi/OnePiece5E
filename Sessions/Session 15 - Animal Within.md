#### 🦹‍♂️ DM Prep: The Soundless Five (Enemy Profiles)

For the next session, here is the squad breakdown:

1. Coda (The Leader): The Conductor. Uses a baton to manipulate teammates' initiative and cast silence.
    
2. Rhythm (The Speedster): Dual-wields daggers. Gets extra attacks if he hits on beat.
    
3. Bass (The Tank): The one who helped cleave the bell. Uses a massive shield and shockwaves.
    
4. Treble (The Sniper): The other bell-cleaver. Uses a rifle that fires high-frequency needles.
    
5. Synth (The Tech): Uses Dials and weird gadgets to create illusions or environmental hazards.
    


###### 1. Bass (The Tank)

The massive man who cleaved the bell. He fights with brute force and heavy armor.

Base Stat Block: KNIGHT (MM p.347)

Medium humanoid, lawful evil

- Armor Class: 18 (Plate Armor)
    
- Hit Points: 52 (8d8 + 16)
    
- Speed: 30 ft.
    
- Challenge: 3 (700 XP)
    

Reflavoring:

- Leadership $\rightarrow$ "Sub-Woofer Pulse": Instead of shouting orders, he hits his chest armor, emitting a low frequency that bolsters his ally (Treble or Synth).
    
- Greatsword $\rightarrow$ "The Gong Hammer": A massive two-handed maul.
    

Actions:

- Multiattack: Makes two melee attacks.
    
- Greatsword (Hammer): Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (2d6 + 3) bludgeoning damage.
    
- Heavy Crossbow: Ranged Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, range 100/400 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d10) piercing damage.
    

Reaction:

- Parry: Adds +2 to AC against one melee attack that would hit him. (Flavor: He catches the blade with his vibrating armored palm).

###### 5. Coda (The Mimic)

The Leader of the Soundless Five. He has eaten the Mimo Mimo no Mi (Mimic Mimic Fruit), allowing him to replicate the abilities of other Devil Fruit users he has observed.

Base Stat Block: WARLOCK OF THE ARCHFEY (VGM p.219) Medium humanoid (human), lawful evil

- Armor Class: 14 (Studded Leather + Dex)
    
- Hit Points: 66 (11d8 + 17) - Buffed slightly for a boss
    
- Speed: 30 ft.
    
- STR 10 (+0), DEX 16 (+3), CON 14 (+2), INT 13 (+1), WIS 12 (+1), CHA 18 (+4)
    
- Skills: Deception +6, Perception +4, Performance +6
    
- Senses: Passive Perception 14
    
- Challenge: 4 (1,100 XP)
    

Devil Fruit Traits:

- The Mimic's Eye: Coda has Advantage on Saving Throws against Spells or Magical Effects (Devil Fruit powers) that he has seen used before.
    
- Fluid Form: Due to copying various "body-altering" fruits in the past, Coda has resistance to Non-Magical Bludgeoning damage (copied from a Rubber or Iron user).
    

Actions:

- Multiattack: Coda makes two attacks with his Conductor's Baton or uses one "Mimicry Art."
    
- Conductor's Baton (Rapier): Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 3) piercing damage.
    
- Mimicry Art (Recharge 5-6): Coda unleashes a powerful move he has permanently memorized from famous pirates/Marines. Roll a d4 to determine which one he uses:
    

1. "Fire Fist" (Ace/Sabo Copy): Casts Burning Hands at 3rd Level (5d6 Fire damage, 15ft cone, Dex Save DC 14).
    
2. "Chop Chop Cannon" (Buggy Copy): Casts Misty Step as a bonus action, then makes two Baton attacks with Advantage (flavor: his arm detaches to strike).
    
3. "Smoke Snake" (Smoker Copy): Casts Fog Cloud centered on himself, but he can see through it.
    
4. "Barrier Crash" (Bartolomeo Copy): Casts Shield (Reaction) or pushes a target 10ft back with a transparent wall (Str Save DC 14).
    

Reactions (The Core Mechanic):

- "I've Seen That Before!": Immediately after a creature Coda can see casts a spell of 1st level or higher, or uses a special class feature (like Tigor’s Rage or Baptiste’s Haki), Coda can use his reaction to copy it.
    

- If it's a Spell/Attack: Coda can cast/use that same ability on his next turn without expending a slot.
    
- If it's a Buff (like Rage): Coda gains the benefits of that feature until the end of his next turn (e.g., if Tigor rages, Coda gains Resistance to S/P/B damage for 1 round).

###### 🧠 DM Tactics for Coda

1. The "Haki" Interaction (Narrative Moment): Since Baptiste used his Haki at the gate (and it was recorded by Sound Dials/Cameras), Coda has "seen" it.

- Round 1 Opener: Coda attempts to use "Mock Haki."
    
- Effect: He casts Fear (3rd Level Spell, Wisdom Save DC 14).
    
- Description: "Coda's eyes flash. He tries to imitate the pressure Baptiste released at the gate. It feels artificial—like a recording played too loud—but it is still terrifying."
    

2. Copying the Party:

- Vs. Tigor: If Tigor uses his Beast Claws (Primal Savagery/Unarmed Strike), Coda's hands will morph into claws for a round, dealing slashing damage.
    
- Vs. Roma: If Roma uses a specific martial art move, Coda mirrors the stance perfectly.
    
- Vs. B.O.B: If B.O.B shoots a laser/projectile, Coda mimics the hand motion and fires an Eldritch Blast beam, claiming he "Copied the robot's fruit" (mocking him).
    

3. Weakness:

- He relies on seeing the target. Blindness or Darkness shuts down his ability to learn new moves (though he keeps his Pre-loaded ones).
