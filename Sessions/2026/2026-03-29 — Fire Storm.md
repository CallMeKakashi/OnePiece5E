# 🎲 Session Outline: _Flames Over Valehart Waters_

## 🎬 ACT 1 — The Aftermath (Roma / Malphas POV)

### **Opening Scene: “Water, Mud, and Mahogany”**

**Goal:** Ground players, reorient after chaos, introduce urgency + tone contrast

- **Roma wakes up** choking as **Bob spits water on him**
- Describe:
    - Soaked floors
    - Mud + sand dragged inside
    - Heavy breathing, exhaustion
- **Malphas** is in the corner recovering

👉 **Knock at the door**

### **Enter Jefferson (Butler of House Valehart)**

- Calm, controlled, slightly annoyed
- Key line tone:
    
    > “That flooring is _mahogany_. Do try to respect it.”
    
- He:
    - Visibly judges the mess
    - Ignores chaos emotionally (contrast humor + tension)
    - Requests:
        
        > “Any able-bodied individuals, follow me. Lord Valehart requires you.”
        

---

### **Transition → Lord Valehart’s Chamber**

**Purpose:** Deliver plot hook + looming threat

- Before full exposition…

---

## 🎭 ACT 2 — Perspective Shift (Ring Bearer POV)

### **Scene: Inner Mind / Aegir Dialogue**

**Goal:** Build dread + foreshadow corruption

- Player hears **Aegir (entity in the rings)**
- Tone:
    - Calm, predatory, inevitable
- Key beats:
    - “I have recovered…”
    - “The pain is only just beginning.”
    - “You will have everything you desire.”

👉 Let player respond freely — this is a _roleplay goldmine_

---

### **Wake-Up Scene (Linus)**

- Linus is **concerned, maybe frustrated**
- Observes rings worsening

**Key lines:**

- “We don’t want another incident.”
- “Why are you back on the ship?”

👉 This implies:

- Past loss of control
- Stakes tied to the rings

---

## 🔁 ACT 3 — Return to Valehart POV

### **Intel Drop (Malphas + Marines Call)**

**Goal:** Introduce main threat: Lunarian woman

- Communication from marine forces:
    - Escort warship destroyed
    - “A flying figure”
    - Fire-based destruction
- Description:
    - Ships scorched
    - Survivors panicking
    - Urgent request for reinforcements

👉 Let players ask questions here

---

## ⚔️ ACT 4 — Mobilization

### **Preparation Phase**

Give players agency:

- Heal
- Plan
- Gear up
- Ask Valehart questions

Optional:

- Valehart gives:
    - Strategic map
    - Warning about Lunarians
    - Stakes (political / personal)

---

## 🚢 ACT 5 — The Battlefield

### **Arrival Scene (Cinematic)**

**Describe this slowly and vividly:**

- One warship:
    - **Half-sunken**
    - Blackened, smoking
- Another:
    - Still fighting
    - Firing volleys into the sky
- In the distance:
    - A **figure flying rapidly**
    - Fire trails cutting through the air

---

### **Important Constraint (Player Control)**

If players try to skip ahead:

- The **front warship fires on them**
- Cannons force engagement

👉 Make it clear:

- They must:
    - Disable cannons OR
    - Board and fight

---

## ⚔️ ACT 6 — Ship 1: First Boss Encounter

### **Environment**

- Deck:
    - Chaotic
    - Marines scrambling
    - Cannons firing
- Smoke + fire + shouting

---

### **Boss: Burn-Scar Rapier User**

**Phase 1: Controlled Duelist**

- Calm, precise
- Uses:
    - Haki slashes (ranged pressure)
    - Mobility
- Doesn’t take players seriously at first

---

### **Adds:**

- Marines (Petty Officer level)
    - Organized
    - Use formations / suppressive fire

---

### **Phase 2 Trigger (Boss < ~50% HP or pressured)**

🔥 **Reveals Flame Logia**

- Body ignites
- Attacks become:
    - AoE fire slashes
    - Burning terrain
- Personality shift:
    - More aggressive / unstable

---

### **Tactical Goals for Players**

- Disable cannons (reduces battlefield pressure)
- Handle marines (action economy control)
- Survive boss escalation

---
