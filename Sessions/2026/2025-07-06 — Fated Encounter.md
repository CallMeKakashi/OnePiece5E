# Session 3 — Fated Encounter

## TL;DR

- The crew arrives at [[Spirit Cliff]] (Marine presence visible: **G-45**) and meets [[Bramble]] at **Bramble & Brothers**, who offers a **Grand Line–capable ship** for a job: steal a **small chest** from the [[Worgoman Bank]] director’s cabin.
- The party builds a heist plan around the **casino** (director is a regular): win through early tables to reach poker, get the director drunk, and lift his keys; Bramble provides **blueprints** and his workers supply **disguises**.
- [[Linus Marrow]] begins examining the **black ring** recovered from [[Kirro King]]’s hideout and invites [[🦾 Saplea D. Isla — “Dreadnaught”|Saplea]] to [[The Armada Sixfolds base of operations|the Armada]] to discuss it.
- A market run turns violent when marines recognize Saplea’s bounty; the crew wins a quick scuffle, stashes bodies, and loots **marine gear** plus a **Den Den Mushi** “info phone” and radio.
- The crew has a tense first encounter with [[Veyl Corven]] and [[Serica Corven]] ([[Sixfold]] siblings with **redacted bounties**); they refuse to help with the casino scheme, but Veyl removes nearby marines to keep heat down.
