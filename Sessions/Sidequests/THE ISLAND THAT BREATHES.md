_A Kaen Solaris Trial for Level 3 Players_

---

# 🌌 **1. ASTRAL INTRO (1–2 minutes)**

Kaen Solaris stands resting both hands atop Icarus’s hilt.

The sapphire shadows whirl into the number **4**.

> **“Your trial lies on an island that breathes.  
> Its life is fading.  
> Decide whether to preserve or end it.”**

A flash of astral blue — the players fall through starry void — _bam!_ they land on…

---

# 🏝️ **2. PUFF-ISLAND ARRIVAL**

### **The island**

Looks like a giant pufferfish, slightly inflating and deflating.  
Vibrant colors, funny-shaped houses, drifting foam bubbles.

### **NPCs that run to them:**

### **Chief Bobo-Breath**

A round puff-fishman fatherly type.

Voice prompt:

> “O–outsiders! P-please… the island… c-can’t breathe!”

### **Dr. Snurff**

Starfish doctor wearing a snorkel.

Voice prompt:

> “Something is choking the airflow tunnels! If they collapse, Puff-Island will sink!”

### **Quest Explanation**

- Puff-Island breathes through **warm tunneling vents** inside it.
    
- Something huge is blocking the central airway.
    
- The island is breathing slower → risks sinking.
    

---

# 🎯 **QUEST OBJECTIVE**

### **Enter the tunnels, clear the obstruction, save (or kill) the creature inside.**

---

# 🧭 **3. ENTERING THE “LUNG TUNNELS” (Dungeon Start)**

The tunnels are warm, rubbery, slightly bouncy.  
They expand and contract with the island’s breaths.

### **Environmental Mechanics:**

- Every 1–2 rounds of exploration, the tunnel inhales/exhales.
    
- Inhale: players are pulled 5 ft inward (DC 10 Str to resist).
    
- Exhale: gust pushes them backward 5 ft (DC 10 Dex to stay standing).
    

Use this for comedy and minor challenge.

---

# 🐟 **4. ENCOUNTER 1: SNEEZEFISH SWARM**

### **Description**

Tiny fish embedded in the walls inflate, sneeze, then blast air.

Use **Swarm of Quippers**, but:

- Damage becomes **bludgeoning**
    
- Replace “Swarm” trait with:
    

### **Air Burst (Recharge 4–6)**

Every creature in the swarm’s space → **DC 12 Dex save**

- Fail: 1d6 bludgeoning + pushed 10 ft
    
- Success: half damage, no push
    

### **Tactics**

- It tries to blow players into pit-like depressions
    
- Never fights to the death; scatters at 10 HP or less
    

---

# 🦀 **5. ENCOUNTER 2: SUCTION CRABS**

### **Description**

Two large crabs with suction-cup claws cling to walls and ceiling.

Use **Giant Crabs**, but add:

### **Suction Grab (On Hit)**

If a crab hits with Claw:  
Target must succeed **DC 12 Athletics/Acrobatics** or becomes **Grappled**.

### **Ceiling Drop Attack**

If players walk beneath them:

- +2 to hit
    
- Deals extra **1d6 bludgeoning**
    

### **Tactics**

- Try to drag players up walls
    
- Run away if reduced below 6 HP
    

---

# 🌬️ **6. PUZZLE ROOM: THE COMPRESSOR CHAMBER**

A large chamber that expands and contracts like a beating lung.

**Goal:**  
Cross the room without getting crushed by contractions.

### **Mechanics:**

Every round:

1. **Expansion (Round 1)**  
    Walls widen – movement normal.
    
2. **Compression (Round 2)**  
    Walls slam inward  
    Creatures must **Dash** or **DC 13 Dex save** or take **2d6 bludgeoning**.
    
3. **Release (Round 3)**  
    Ground launches a gentle gust – players must **DC 10 Dex save** or fall prone.
    

Repeat cycle.

### **Solution Options:**

- Players time movements
    
- Use climbing gear
    
- Use abilities (Step of the Wind, enhance movement, etc.)
    

---

# 🐄 **7. BOSS ROOM: BABY SEA COW**

_CR 2 (reskinned Giant Boar)_

Large, adorable, panicking sea cow stuck in the warm tunnel, mucus everywhere.

It sees the players → bellows loudly → becomes hostile only out of fear.

---

# 🐄 **BABY SEA COW — STAT BLOCK (CR 2)**

_Large Beast, Unaligned_

**AC:** 12  
**HP:** 42 (5d10+15)  
**Speed:** 40 ft., swim 30 ft.

**STR** 17 / **DEX** 10 / **CON** 16  
**INT** 2 / **WIS** 7 / **CHA** 5

### **Traits**

**Panicked Bulk (Reaction)**  
When the sea cow takes damage:  
It moves half its speed and creatures must succeed **DC 13 Dex save** or take **7 (2d4+3)** bludgeoning & fall prone.

### **Actions**

**Body Slam**  
+5 to hit | 5 ft reach  
Hit: **10 (2d8+3)** bludgeoning

**Thrash (Recharge 5–6)**  
Creature flails wildly.  
All creatures within 10 ft must **DC 13 Dex**

- Fail: **9 (2d6+3)** bludgeoning + prone
    
- Success: Half damage, no prone
    

### **AI Tactics**

- It tries to escape rather than kill
    
- It slams into walls in panic
    
- If reduced to half HP → stops fighting if calmed
    

### **Calming Options**

- Animal Handling DC 14
    
- Food bribe
    
- Talking softly (with crew RP)
    
- Devil Fruit trickery
    
- Dr. Snurff’s advice (Players can recall him mentioning: “Sea cows love humming!”)
    

If calmed, the mucus clears as it relaxes.

---

# ❤️ **8. ONCE THE SEA COW IS FREED**

The tunnels rumble. Airflow resumes.  
The island begins breathing normally again.

Villagers celebrate.

---

# ⚖️ **9. THE CHOICE**

### **GOOD ENDING — Save the baby sea cow**

They escort it outside as a friend.

Chief Bobo-Breath tearfully cheers:

> “You saved Puff-Island!”

Reward:

- **Air Dial** (1/day Gust)
    
- 150–200 berries
    
- Feast + comedic celebration scene
    

Kaen Solaris in the Astral Plane:

> **“Compassion born from strength is rare.  
> You honored the breath of life.”**

---

### **EVIL ENDING — Kill it**

The island breathes again…  
But villagers stare in despair.

Dr. Snurff:

> “You… you didn’t have to kill it…”

They get:

- **Suction Crab Gauntlet** (advantage on grapples)
    
- 100 berries
    
- Villagers look away in fear
    
- Solaris speaks sternly:
    

> **“You chose efficiency over empathy.  
> The stars remember such choices.”**

---

# 📜 **10. QUEST COMPLETION**

Whether noble or ruthless, players return to their world with no memory — unless Kaen summons them again.
