## ⚔️ Sidequest: **“Blood and Lumafang”**

**Setting:** A few years ago — before the events of _Blood and Brine._  
**Location:** The _Underdocks of Port Tenebra_, a lawless marine blacksite masquerading as a shipyard and fighting pit.  
**Objective:** Expose and shut down the illegal serum trade, retrieve a sample of Lumafang, or destroy it completely.

---

## 🧩 Narrative Setup

### The Lumafang Serum

A prototype offshoot of **Project Wonderland**, synthesized from Sea King blood and rare minerals. It heightens aggression and reflexes, giving temporary “predatory” traits (glowing eyes, muscle strain, heat shimmer). The side effects are madness, nerve burn, and blood rot.

Rumor says a **Marine Captain** named _Reddan Korr_ has been funneling samples to bounty hunters in exchange for “field testing” — using fighters as lab rats in the ring.

---

## 🎭 Act I – The Pit Beneath the Port

**Scene Setup:**  
Malak arrives undercover as a fighter to infiltrate the ring, gathering proof of the marine-bounty hunter trade. Shako — tracing rumors of his brother’s friend’s disappearance and the spread of a “rage serum” in coastal towns — attends the fight to investigate.

**Scenes:**

- The fighting pit is lit with molten glass lanterns, sweat and blood thick in the air.
    
- The reigning champion, **Malak**, is introduced with crowd chants and brutal respect.
    
- Between fights, Shako spots a hooded marine exchanging crates marked _“for medical disposal”_ — Lumafang samples.

**Key NPCs:**

- **Fixer Reddan Korr** (Marine Captain, corrupt, runs the ring through intermediaries).
    
- **Zeff**, an old fighter turned announcer, recognizes Shako’s name and warns him to “stay out of the deep water.”
    

**Tension Point:**  
The fight promoter injects one of the fighters with Lumafang to make the crowd go wild. The fighter goes berserk mid-match — glowing veins, shark-like teeth, and bone spurs. Shako intervenes, crossing paths with Malak.

**Result:**  
A reluctant alliance forms as they realize they share a goal — end the trade.

---

## 💥 Act II – The Smuggler’s Den

**Objective:** Infiltrate the back tunnels beneath the fighting pit — old sewer docks where crates are loaded onto small marine ships.

**Encounters:**

- **Bounty Hunters (4–5)** testing Lumafang doses, gaining short-lived buffs but erratic behavior (reckless attacks, HP burn).
    
- A mini-boss: **Serum Enforcer**, a fighter permanently mutated from overuse (multiattack, regen until fire/acid is used).
    
- Environmental challenge: unstable tunnels with glowing veins of liquid light (residual Lumafang runoff, explosive if ignited).
    

**Discovery:**  
They find a ledger linking **Reddan Korr** to “Mugen Supply Channel D3.”  
Optional: a damaged vial of Lumafang — volatile but valuable.

---

## 🔥 Act III – Captain of the Pit

**Boss:** **Marine Captain Reddan Korr (CR 8)**

- Uses experimental Lumafang injector gauntlets.
    
- When bloodied, injects himself — gaining feral traits and a berserk phase (rage aura, disadvantage on Wisdom saves).
    
- His dialogue reveals he believes the serum could “revolutionize the Marines — soldiers who _never stop fighting_.”
    

**Arena:**  
The final fight happens on the ship’s deck as it’s being loaded — half aflame, surrounded by cheering fighters unsure who to root for.

**Resolution Options:**

- **Destroy the Serum:** Ship explodes — righteous ending, both barely escape.
    
- **Steal a Vial:** Sets up future arcs — this sample resurfaces in _Project Wonderland_.
    
- **Capture Korr:** He’s silenced before revealing who in the Marines funded him (tie to Cipher Pol or Germa).
    

---

## 🩸 Epilogue

- **Malak’s Outcome:** Gains a reputation for tearing down the fighting pit — earns Blackhand Cane’s attention, leading to his recruitment.
    
- **Shako’s Outcome:** Returns to his brother’s network with proof of marine corruption — and nightmares of what the serum did to men.
    
- **Future Connection:** In the present day, traces of Lumafang are detected again — refined, mass-produced — tying into the Wonderland arc.
