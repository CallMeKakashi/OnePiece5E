### Brawling Style

At 3rd level, you settle on a specific brawling style to define your brawling: Daredevil, Fishman Karate, Ninja, Six Powers Master, Okama Kenpo, Sword Sage, Sumo Wrestler, Open Hand, Way of Mercy, Drunken Master, Chromatic Commandment, Private Eye, and Expressionist, all detailed at the end of the class description.
Your fighting style grants you features at 3rd level and then again at 6th, 11th, and 17th level.
