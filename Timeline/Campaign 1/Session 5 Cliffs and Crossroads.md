## **Session 5: Cliffs and Crossroads**

_The final stop before Reverse Mountain._

---

### 🗺️ Scene 1: “A Ship That Can’t Climb”

**Location:** Open sea, nearing Reverse Mountain  
**Time:** Morning; mist clings to the waves, the wind howls louder by the minute

As the shape of **Reverse Mountain** looms through the mist, a realization sets in: _this ship won't survive the climb_.

**Veyl** stands atop the mast, cloak whipping behind him.

> “That current up ahead... it doesn’t just carry ships. It _launches_ them. If the hull gives out mid-air, I can’t guarantee I’ll catch everyone. Not with Serica nearby. I’m not risking her—or any of you.”

He pauses, eyes scanning the mist, then looks down seriously.

> “I’ll scout ahead. There might be an island where you can find something sturdier. You’re wanted folk now, right? Bet you've got the bounty to back it up.”

Without waiting for a reply, **Veyl vanishes**—a blur across the sea, sprinting atop the waves like a phantom.

---

### 🛟 Scene 2: “Serica’s Watch”

**Location:** Deck of the drifting ship  
**Time:** Veyl is gone, the wind calmer

**Serica** takes Veyl’s usual perch near the helm, arms behind her head, watching the crew with an amused smile.

> “Don’t mind him. He gets twitchy when he’s worried. Especially about me.”

She seems more relaxed than usual. Invite the crew to talk to her. She’s candid, sarcastic, and curious—asks about their dreams, regrets, even favorite drinks—but dodges anything too personal.

Let roleplay flow here. Maybe she lets something slip.

---

### 🏝️ Scene 3: “Spirit Cliffs Island”

**Time:** An hour later

**Veyl returns**, spraying mist as he skids across the sea beside the ship.

> “Found something. Big island. _Spirit Cliffs._ Looks like your best shot.”

He gestures ahead.

> “There’s a Marine base by the docks—low security, but don’t go flashing those wanted posters. Still... you’ve got options.”

### 🛠️ Scene 5: “Bramble’s Deal”

**Location:** Bramble & Sons Shipyard

A bell clangs as the party enters a cluttered yard of half-finished hulls and sawdust.

**Bramble**—a scarred, broad-shouldered man with a jaw like cracked granite—wipes grease from his hands.

> “What are you lot sailing? A rowboat?”  
> “You want Grand Line-ready? I’ve got three options—and none of ‘em cheap.”

He offers:

1. **Buy a ship outright** – ~1 million berries
    
2. **Trade your current ship and do a favor** – pirates are attacking a nearby supply line
    
3. **“Borrow” or “win” the money** – he nods toward the Casino and the Bank
    

He tears off a ticket and hands it over.

> “You’ll find me drinking at The Salted Bone. Decide fast. I’m not a patient man.”

---

### 🥃 Scene 6: “The Salted Bone Encounter”

**Location:** The Salted Bone Tavern  
**Time:** Evening

The place is chaos: shouting, laughter, clinking mugs. **Bramble** is slumped at the bar, already a few drinks in.

In a shadowed booth, a group of **ruffians** is cornering a **hooded figure**. One grabs her wrist.

If the crew interferes, a bar fight breaks out—or a clever conversation might turn the tide.

When revealed, the figure is a woman with a noble bearing beneath her ragged cloak—**sharp violet eyes**, silver rings glinting beneath her hood.

> “I’m **Princess Aralia Cindross**, of the **Virellis Kingdom**. I’m avoiding… suitors. And the people who sent them.”

She looks around.

> “If the Marines find me here, I’m dead.”

Let players choose how to help—or if they’ll get involved at all.

## 🔍 Optional Scenes

### 🧱 Scene 7: “Marine Base Infiltration” _(Optional)_

**Goal:** Sneak in to steal supplies, documents, or perhaps a ship blueprint.  
**Complication:** A suspicious Commodore and security snails.

This is perfect for stealth or infiltration-heavy players. Could uncover intel about Aralia—or bounties on the crew.

---

### 🏴‍☠️ Scene 8: “The Pirate Job” _(Optional)_

**Objective:** Bramble’s favor—take out a pirate crew harassing local supply ships.  
**Twist:** The pirates may offer the crew a deal: steal from the bank or casino instead.

Combat-heavy or diplomacy-based, depending on how the party plays it. Also a chance to earn unexpected allies or enemies.

---

### 🎰 Scene 9: “Black Tide Den – Casino Shenanigans” _(Optional)_

**Objective:** Win the ship money... or lose it.  
**Gimmick:** Rigged games, suspicious pit bosses, and a rumored Devil Fruit as a high-stakes prize.

Could lead to a full-on heist or clever con. Or a deal with a shady underworld broker.

---

### 🏛️ Scene 10: “World Government Bank” _(Optional)_

**Objective:** Take a loan—or rob the place.  
**Obstacle:** Heavy protection, ciphered ledgers, and snails that record every voice.

Could tie into the Marine Base or Casino if players want a more elaborate setup. A clean getaway here is nearly impossible.
