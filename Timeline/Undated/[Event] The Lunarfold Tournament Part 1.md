# The Lunarfold Tournament (Part 1)

Shipboard **crew-ranking tournament** after the G-45 facility (Episode 8). [[Sixfold]] operatives [[Veyl Corven]] and [[Serica Corven]] treated as full crew; captain rank fixed — bracket determines pecking order below captain. Rules: **no killing**; otherwise unrestricted (including ally interference).

## Beats

1. **Downtime (~3 days)** — recovery, devil-fruit talk, [[Baptiste]] speed training with Veyl.
2. **Tournament called** — deck fights; non-lethal only.
3. **Results (partial)** — [[Serica Corven]] beats [[Roma]] (adjudicated); [[Malphas]] advances through [[Baptiste]] duel; Malphas vs Serica cut mid-fight; **Serica ~3rd** teased; finals in Part 2 ([[Episode 9 - The Lunarfold Tournament Part 2]]).
