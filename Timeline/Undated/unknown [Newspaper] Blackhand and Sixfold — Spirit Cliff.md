# East Blue Herald — Blackhand & Sixfold Conspiracy Rocks Spirit Cliff

**By Morgans — East Blue Correspondent**

## BLACKHAND & SIXFOLD CONSPIRACY ROCKS SPIRIT CLIFF!

**TREASURY RAID SPARKS WIDESPREAD CHAOS — MARINES HOLD THE LINE!**

The tranquil island of Spirit Cliff has been shaken to its core following a violent plot orchestrated by the **Blackhand unit**—a faction of the dreaded Blackhand Pirates—now confirmed to be colluding with the infamous mercenary group **Sixfold.**

The criminals infiltrated a casino, assaulting respected branch director **Halwald** with poisoned drink and stealing classified keys to the **World Government Bank.** Using these keys, they launched a coordinated midnight raid on the Treasury Branch, spreading terror through the district.

As the heist unfolded, Sixfold agents **Veyl** and **Serica**—long pardoned for their so-called "service" in **Verrelies**—emerged not to defend the people, but to shield these pirates. Witnesses report that the Sixfold burned valuable government documents inside the bank vault, destroying records critical to maintaining the island's lawful financial order. **Dr. Linus Marrow** a former member of the Sixfold was also identified to be present on the scene.

Adding insult to injury, the traitorous Sixfold then **joined forces with the Blackhand unit,** driving back Marine Captain **Vorro's** forces through sheer chaos and violence.

During the battle, one of the Blackhand unit, identified as **Baptiste,** revealed his true nature as a **Devil Fruit user**. In a reckless display of power, Baptiste triggered catastrophic malfunctions in the new **G-45 Enforcer Armors,** manufactured by **Mugen Industries** causing multiple Marine casualties as helmets exploded during combat. These actions show the lengths these pirates will go to just for treasure. In the aftermath, the Marines have **halted all contracts with Mugen Industries**, a move that could shake the East Blue military supply chain.

The Blackhand unit, alongside the Sixfold, escaped Spirit Cliff with stolen wealth, including funds looted from the casino vault.

Casino owner **Lucien** had this to say:  
"They poisoned my wine, humiliated my guests, and robbed me blind. These aren't liberators—they're common criminals hiding behind flashy names."

From the streets of SpiritCliff, however, the voices of everyday citizens tell a more complicated tale:

- **Blacksmith, Jorn Pell:** _"Linn fixed my arm after a bad fall. Never asked for a coin, just made sure I could work again. Best doctor this side of the East Blue, I swear."_
- **Dockworker, Elias Korrin:** _"Veyl hauled crates with me when half my crew was sick. Didn't ask for a coin, just wanted a smoke. Doesn't sound like the monster the posters say."_
- **Fishmonger, Mara Teel:** _"Serica helped me chase off some lads roughing up the stalls. She never asked for anything back."_
- **Innkeeper, Old Nessa:** _"Sixfold kept to themselves, but they paid their tabs and even patched a leaking roof for me once. Can't say the same for the Navy boys, who drink and leave me short."_

Despite overwhelming odds and sabotage from within, Captain Vorro stood resolute, rallying his forces and minimizing civilian casualties in what could have been a massacre. In an official statement, he declared:

"The people are deceived. Acts of charity mean nothing when weighed against piracy. These are criminals, plain and simple — and they will be treated as such. These outlaws will not escape justice. The Blackhand Pirates and Sixfold are enemies of the people, and I will dedicate my strength to hunting them down until every last one is put in chains."

In the aftermath, Branch Director **Halwald** has been detained for his alleged connections to debt-based coercion. However, Marine sources stress that his corruption in no way justifies the pirates' actions. Instead, it highlights how easily lawless forces like the Blackhand unit exploit weak links to destabilize order.

With the Blackhand unit fleeing into the mist and the Sixfold betraying their pardon, Marine Intelligence has restored full bounties on all conspirators. The message from Marineford is clear: **piracy, treachery, and collusion will never be tolerated.**
