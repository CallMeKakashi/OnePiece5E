# North Blue Herald — Plasmoid Turns Frostwind to Ashes

**By Daniels — North Blue Correspondent**

## THE PLASMOID TURNS FROSTWIND TO ASHES

Last night, the notorious shapeshifter known as **Droven Calligos** the right hand man of the **Wolfkin pirates** a fleet under **Blackhand Pirates** staged a brazen act that has thrown the entire North Blue into chaos. Witnesses report that under the cover of heavy snow, Droven infiltrated a Marine supply flotilla anchored near Frostwind.

At first light, the harbor was rocked by explosions—barrels of black powder ignited in the Marine depots. Eyewitnesses claim they saw "a man with no face" slipping through cannon fire, dissolving locks and hulls alike with a burning acid. By the time reinforcements arrived, the Marine flagship's mast had collapsed into the bay, flames licking the ice floes.

Most shocking was the aftermath: **a Marine patrol chart meant for Reverse Mountain was stolen and publicly burned in the town square** before Droven vanished again into the blizzard.

The incident forced Vice Admiral Kuren to redeploy three squadrons originally assigned to patrol the Reverse Mountain crossing.

With tensions at their peak, Frostwind now bears the scars of yet another battle in the escalating war between the Navy and the lawless tides of the North Blue.
