# North Blue Herald — Navy Commander Slain on Frosthaven

**By Daniels — North Blue Correspondent**

## NAVY COMMANDER SLAIN IN COLD BLOOD – PIRATES AND BOUNTY HUNTERS CLASH ON FROSTHAVEN

Frosthaven has become the scene of bloodshed after a routine Navy investigation spiraled into chaos. According to official Navy reports, Commander Svarn and his men were dispatched to the island to investigate strange anomalies at the mountain peak. What began as a peaceful mission ended in tragedy.

While attempting to spare the innocent natives from conflict, Commander Svarn was ambushed by two notorious Oni criminals—Dorian and Saphira, both wanted for prior acts of violence across the North Blue. In a brutal and cowardly assault, several brave sailors lost their lives.

Worse still, unconfirmed reports suggest the Blackhand Pirates may have had a rogue unit in play, interfering directly with Navy operations. Eyewitness accounts place a mysterious figure—possibly tied to the feared Second Fleet Commander, Goro the Maneater—among the chaos.

Adding fuel to the fire, the infamous bounty hunter known as Kanto the Avaricious has allegedly aligned himself with these criminals. Once seen as a man who upheld order through his trade, Kanto's reputation has been tarnished with whispers of betrayal.

Eira Snowheart, acting chief of Frosthaven's native tribes, gave this heated statement:

"Yeah, the bounty hunter seems to know the guy… what was his name? Droven or something. Piece of—"

(The remainder of her remarks were deemed unfit for publication.)

Villagers have further claimed to witness two more unidentified figures traveling with these known criminals. While their names and appearances remain uncertain, sources suggest these men are just as dangerous—if not more—than the pirates they accompany.

The Navy has vowed justice for Commander Svarn. Additional patrols have been dispatched across the North Blue, and fresh bounties have been issued for the capture of these fugitives.

North Blue must remain vigilant.
