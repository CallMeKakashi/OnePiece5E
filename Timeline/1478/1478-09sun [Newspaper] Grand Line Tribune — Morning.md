# Grand Line Tribune — 9th Sun, 1478 (Morning)

*"Truth Rides the Waves" — Price: 2,000 Beli*

## COUP ON DECIBELLA

**Young Lord Riff Sin Seizes Throne**

In one of the most shocking developments of the month, the revolution on Decibella has escalated into a full overnight coup.

The uprising was reportedly led by **Riff Sin**, the young heir of House Sin, who has now declared himself ruler of the island.

Sources claim the coup was aided by the infamous **Lunarfold Pirates**.

According to eyewitness accounts:

- The elite royal guard of the former king were slaughtered.
- Loyalist forces were overwhelmed during the night.
- Surviving nobles were either captured or executed.

A high-ranking noble witness, **Lady Soefra Anthem**, has reportedly fled the island with several other refugees.

Lady Anthem and her companions are believed to be hiding on an undisclosed island after escaping shortly before the executions began.

Marine forces are already mobilizing to assess the situation.

Commodore Briggs of the G-4.5 unit has personally taken command of the investigation. Officials are particularly concerned that the coup could disrupt the former king's negotiations to bring Decibella into the World Government.

In light of the incident, new bounty increases for members of the Lunarfold Pirates have been issued. Updated posters accompany this edition.

---

## SHADOWS' SOUTH BLUE STRONGHOLD DESTROYED

**Sixfolds Accused — Bounties Updated**

In a devastating strike reported late last night, a major South Blue installation belonging to the bounty hunter guild The Shadows has been completely destroyed.

Initial reports indicate the attack was carried out by members of the mercenary order The Sixfolds.

Guildmaster Jiro issued an angry statement shortly after news broke:

> This attack was completely unprovoked.  
> The Sixfolds will pay for these crimes.

Witnesses describe a coordinated assault that leveled the compound in less than an hour. Surviving agents claim the attackers appeared to be searching for specific targets before destroying the facility.

In response, Marine Headquarters has issued updated bounty posters for known Sixfold operatives. The revised posters are included with this issue of the Tribune.

---

## VELLENCRUZ SUCCESSION ANNOUNCED

**Baron Lazarus Assumes Authority**

Following the sudden death of Baron Laziel Vellencruz, leadership of the Vellencruz estate has passed to his second son.

The newly appointed **Baron Lazarus Vellencruz** has formally assumed control of the family's holdings on Driftroot Island.

According to government sources, Baron Lazarus is expected to attend the upcoming 100th trial of Lex Imperia, where his presence is said to represent continued cooperation between Driftroot's ruling families and the World Government.

Officials have described the visit as a gesture of "good faith and stability following recent events."

---

## PIRATE ALLIANCE COLLAPSES

**Spider's Nest Crew and Tusk Pirates Split**

The fragile alliance between the Spider's Nest Pirates and the Tusk Pirates has officially dissolved after a violent internal dispute.

Sources close to both crews confirm that casualties were sustained on both sides during the confrontation.

While details remain unclear, multiple informants claim the incident was triggered by Daniel, navigator of the Spider's Nest Pirates.

The exact cause of the conflict remains under investigation.
