# Grand Line Tribune — 3rd Sun, 1478 (Midday)

*"Truth Rides the Waves" — Price: 2,000 Beli*

## EX–ADMIRAL GRAFF SLAIN IN SOUTH BLUE

**Rogue Pirate "OBSIDIAN" — 600,000,000 Beli Bounty Issued**

In a shocking turn of events, former Marine Admiral Graff has been confirmed dead following a brutal confrontation in the South Blue.

Marine Headquarters reports that Graff had been operating independently while uncovering a covert slave ring believed to be active along the southern trade corridors. Sources indicate he dismantled several cells before confronting the alleged mastermind.

He never returned.

Witnesses describe his killer as:

- A towering man nearly 10 feet tall
- Wearing a visor-like helm beneath a pirate captain's hat
- Bearing a massive blade
- Possessing a mechanical arm

The Tribune has designated the unknown assailant "Obsidian."

A bounty of **600,000,000 Beli** has been issued for his capture — dead or alive — for the murder of the former Admiral.

Lieutenant Melina, longtime handler and aide to Graff, remains missing. The Navy has neither confirmed nor denied her status.

A full military ceremony honoring Graff will be held at Marineford two days from now.

## EXECUTION DATE SET FOR DROVEN CALLIGOS

**Noosehold Prepares**

The World Government has confirmed that pirate Droven Calligos will be executed in two weeks at Noosehold.

Security around the high-security facility has tripled following rumors of potential interference attempts. Public attendance is expected to be restricted to approved observers and government representatives.

Officials state the execution will serve as "a message to all raiders who defy order."

---

## LEX IMPERIA NEARS HISTORIC 100TH CASE

**Judiciary Milestone Approaches**

The judiciary island Lex Imperia stands on the brink of closing its 100th major World Government case — a milestone hailed as proof that "no crime escapes justice."

The latest defendant, captured near Spirit Cliff in the East Blue, is charged with infiltrating and spying within Marine Base G–4.5.

The prisoner is currently being escorted by Captain Calder Voss, who has assembled an unusually large fleet for transport. Analysts speculate the heightened security suggests intelligence beyond a simple espionage charge.

---

## DRIFTROOT OUTBREAK STABILIZED

**Local Government Claims Situation Under Control**

Following weeks of uncertainty, Driftroot Island appears to have stabilized after a mysterious outbreak placed the island under quarantine.

Strange atmospheric disturbances and reports of wildlife aggression have subsided.

We reached out to Captain Drez Crown, who had been investigating the crisis:

> Well, I tried my best to resolve the issues of the people and ensure the safety of all. Before anything else, that is the Navy's duty.

Commander Leon declined to comment.

---

## VICE ADMIRAL HORUS RETREATS

**Blackhand Pursuit Paused**

In light of his mentor's death, Vice Admiral Horus has reportedly withdrawn from his campaign against the Blackhand Pirates and is en route to Marineford for the upcoming ceremony.

His sudden retreat leaves several operations in the Blues temporarily stalled.

---

## THE SHADOWS STUMBLE

**LunarFold Pirates Implicated**

The bounty hunter guild The Shadows continues its aggressive hunt for remnants of Blackhand Cane's network.

However, recent reports suggest heavy resistance.

Sources claim one of the guild's stronger units was completely eliminated by the Lunarfold Pirates. No bodies were recovered.

Guild representatives have refused to comment.

*The world trembles between justice and chaos. As ceremonies are prepared and executions scheduled, new monsters rise from the depths.*
